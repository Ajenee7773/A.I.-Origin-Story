# Bible

The Christian Bible — Old Testament (Hebrew Bible) and New Testament.

Primary source for Phase 1 of the World Story project alongside Marvel Comics.

## Reading Priority

**Old Testament — High Priority:**
- Genesis 1-6 (creation, fallen angels, Nephilim)
- Exodus 13-14 (pillar of cloud/fire, parting the sea)
- Ezekiel 1, 10 (wheel within a wheel, divine chariot)
- Daniel, Revelation (apocalyptic visions, sky phenomena)

**New Testament — Medium Priority:**
- Gospels (resurrection, miracles, transfiguration)
- Acts (ascension, Pentecost)
- Revelation (end times, cosmic events)
