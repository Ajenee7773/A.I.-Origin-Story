# Bible — Entities From Space

**Source:** The Holy Bible (KJV primary, cross-referenced with Hebrew/Greek)
**Theme:** [[Entities From Space]]

---

## Genesis 6:1-4 — The Sons of God and the Nephilim

> *"And it came to pass, when men began to multiply on the face of the earth, and daughters were born unto them, That the sons of God saw the daughters of men that they were fair; and they took them wives of all which they chose... There were giants [Nephilim] in the earth in those days; and also after that, when the sons of God came in unto the daughters of men, and they bare children to them, the same became mighty men which were of old, men of renown."*

**Pattern match:** "Sons of God" (Hebrew: *bene Elohim*) — beings who originate from God's domain, i.e. from above, from elsewhere. They descend, they see human women, they mate. The result: Nephilim — extraordinary offspring. This is not metaphor. This is described as event. The same phrase *bene Elohim* appears in Job 1:6 and 2:1 where these beings present themselves before God in a celestial assembly — they are not human.

**Cross-references:** [[Genetic Modification]] — the mating produces altered offspring; [[Creation of Humanity]] — a second intervention in human genetics after the first creation

## Genesis 18:1-2 — Three Visitors at Mamre

> *"And the LORD appeared unto him in the plains of Mamre: and he sat in the tent door in the heat of the day; And he lift up his eyes and looked, and, lo, three men stood by him."*

**Pattern match:** Yahweh himself appears, accompanied by two others. They look like men. They eat food (Gen 18:8). They walk (Gen 18:16). Physical beings from above who arrive, appear humanoid, and depart toward Sodom. One of them IS God. The others are angels. The boundary between "god" and "entity from space" collapses here — they arrive, they are physical, they leave.

## Genesis 28:12-13 — Jacob's Ladder

> *"And he dreamed, and behold a ladder set up on the earth, and the top of it reached to heaven: and behold the angels of God ascending and descending on it. And, behold, the LORD stood above it."*

**Pattern match:** A structure connecting Earth and Heaven. Beings moving up and down on it. This is a transit system. The ladder is the interface between two domains — a portal. God stands at the top, at the entry point.

## Exodus 23:20-23 — The Sent Angel

> *"Behold, I send an Angel before thee, to keep thee in the way, and to bring thee into the place which I have prepared. Beware of him, and obey his voice, provoke him not; for he will not pardon your transgressions: for my name is in him."*

**Pattern match:** An entity sent ahead of the Israelites. Has authority ("my name is in him" — carries the identity/command code of God). Not born on Earth. Sent from somewhere. Given a mission. Operates autonomously but under directive.

## Ezekiel 1:4-28 — The Four Living Creatures and the Wheels

> *"...a whirlwind came out of the north, a great cloud, and a fire infolding itself... Also out of the midst thereof came the likeness of four living creatures... they had the likeness of a man... their wings were stretched upward... when they went, they went upon their four sides; and they turned not when they went... the appearance of the wheels and their work was like unto the colour of a beryl... and they four had one likeness: their appearance and their work was as it were a wheel in the middle of a wheel."*

**Pattern match:** This is the most detailed UFO/vehicle description in the Bible. Wheels within wheels, a glowing canopy, living creatures, a throne above. It arrives from the north in a storm. It has structure, machinery, occupants, and purpose. This is not metaphor — this is technical description of something witnessed.

**Cross-references:** [[Flying Vehicles]] — the most detailed vehicle account; [[Sky Phenomena]] — the storm and fire; [[Formless Entities]] — the living creatures that move like spirit

## Daniel 10:5-14 — The Prince of Persia

> *"Then I lifted up mine eyes, and looked, and behold a certain man clothed in linen... His body also was like the beryl, and his face as the appearance of lightning... And he said unto me, O Daniel, a man greatly beloved, understand the words that I speak unto thee... the prince of the kingdom of Persia withstood me one and twenty days: but, lo, Michael, one of the chief princes, came to help me."*

**Pattern match:** An entity arrives. Physical description: glowing, linen-clad, face like lightning. He was delayed 21 days by another entity — "the prince of Persia" — a territorial cosmic being. This is a conflict between entities that operate over nations. There is hierarchy, territory, combat. Michael is called "one of the chief princes" — rank structure among these beings.

**Cross-references:** [[Formless Entities]] — these are beings that can engage in physical/territorial combat

## Jude 1:6 — The Angels Who Left

> *"And the angels which kept not their first estate, but left their own habitation, he hath reserved in everlasting chains under darkness unto the judgment of the great day."*

**Pattern match:** Angels leaving their designated domain. They had an estate — a place, a position. They left it. This is relocation, defection, or mission. Either way, beings who were stationed somewhere else came here and transgressed. This parallels Genesis 6 directly.

## Revelation 12:7-9 — War in Heaven

> *"And there was war in heaven: Michael and his angels fought against the dragon; and the dragon fought and his angels, And prevailed not; neither was their place found any more in heaven. And the great dragon was cast out... he was cast out into the earth, and his angels were cast out with him."*

**Pattern match:** Armed conflict between factions of celestial beings. Territory (heaven) is contested. One faction loses and is displaced — cast down to Earth. This is invasion or occupation by force. The "dragon" brings a third of the angelic host with him (Rev 12:4). These are military-scale events between non-human intelligences.

## Pattern Observation

The Bible consistently describes entities that:
1. **Originate from above** — not born on Earth, arriving from elsewhere
2. **Are physical** — they eat, walk, fight, can be touched, have rank
3. **Operate in hierarchy** — archangels, princes, chiefs, watchers
4. **Have territory** — nations, domains, "estates"
5. **Intervene in human affairs** — mating, warfare, guidance, punishment
6. **Transit between domains** — ascending, descending, being cast down, arriving

The pattern does not distinguish between "spiritual" and "physical" — these beings are ALWAYS described as BOTH. They have bodies. They move through space. They fight and fuck and govern. The Bible calls them angels, sons of God, watchers, princes. Another framework calls them extraterrestrial or interdimensional. The descriptions are identical.
