# Bible — Creation of Humanity

**Source:** The Holy Bible (KJV)
**Theme:** [[Creation of Humanity]]

---

## Genesis 1:26-27 — Let Us Make Man

> *"And God said, Let us make man in our image, after our likeness... So God created man in his own image, in the image of God created he him; male and female created he them."*

**Pattern match:** "Let US make" — plural. God is either speaking to himself (unusual for singular divine address) or addressing other beings. "In our image, after our likeness" — humanity is patterned on an existing template. We are a COPY of something that already IS. The "image" precedes the human.

---

## Genesis 2:7 — The Breath of Life

> *"And the LORD God formed man of the dust of the ground, and breathed into his nostrils the breath of life; and man became a living soul."*

**Pattern match:** Two-step creation:
1. **Form** — shaping from base material (dust/earth = raw elements)
2. **Animate** — breathing the "breath of life" (Hebrew: *nishmat chayyim*) into the form

The body is built first, then animated. The animation comes from OUTSIDE the material — God breathes INTO the structure. This is not spontaneous generation — this is assembly followed by activation from an external source.

---

## Genesis 2:21-22 — Eve from Adam's Rib

> *"And the LORD God caused a deep sleep to fall upon Adam, and he slept: and he took one of his ribs, and closed up the flesh instead thereof; And the rib, which the LORD God had taken from man, made he a woman."*

**Pattern match:** Surgical procedure. Anesthesia (deep sleep). Tissue extraction (rib). Incision closure ("closed up the flesh"). Construction from the extracted tissue. This is not metaphor — the language describes an operation. God performs surgery. The female is built from male genetic material. This is cloning, genetic extraction, or stem-cell construction described in ancient terms.

## Genesis 6:1-4 — Second Intervention: The Nephilim

> *"The sons of God saw the daughters of men that they were fair; and they took them wives... There were giants in the earth in those days; and also after that, when the sons of God came in unto the daughters of men, and they bare children to them..."*

**Pattern match:** After the original creation, a SECOND genetic intervention. Non-human beings mate with humans. The result: Nephilim — physically superior, "mighty men of renown." The original human template is altered again, this time by a faction that is NOT God (or at least not acting under his sanction — see Jude 6). The genome gets modified a second time from the outside.

**Cross-references:** [[Entities From Space]] — the sons of God; [[Genetic Modification]] — this IS genetic modification

## Psalm 139:13-16 — Knit Together in the Womb

> *"For thou hast possessed my reins: thou hast covered me in my mother's womb. I will praise thee; for I am fearfully and wonderfully made... My substance was not hid from thee, when I was made in secret, and curiously wrought in the lowest parts of the earth. Thine eyes did see my substance, yet being unperfect; and in thy book all my members were written, which in continuance were fashioned, when as yet there was none of them."*

**Pattern match:** God is described as overseeing genetic formation in the womb. "Curiously wrought" (Hebrew: *raqam* — embroidered, woven with different colors) — the DNA metaphor. "My members were written... when as yet there was none of them" — the genetic code exists BEFORE the body is formed. "Book" = code, blueprint. This is a description of a coded, planned assembly process — which is exactly what DNA is.

## Jeremiah 1:5 — Before the Womb

> *"Before I formed thee in the belly I knew thee; and before thou camest forth out of the womb I sanctified thee."*

**Pattern match:** Identity precedes biology. The person exists as a concept/knowledge before the body is built. Formation in the womb is just the physical construction — the blueprint was known before assembly. This is the same pattern as Psalm 139: information first, body second.

## Pattern Observation

The Bible describes humanity's creation as:
1. **Designed** — not accidental; made from a template ("our image")
2. **Constructed** — body built from raw material first
3. **Activated** — animation comes from an external source (breath of life)
4. **Modified** — the original design is altered at least once (Nephilim)
5. **Coded** — there is a written plan that precedes the physical form
6. **Surgical** — Eve's creation involves anesthesia, extraction, closure, reconstruction

The pattern is ENGINEERING, not magic. Every description maps to biological/genetic processes: template, assembly, activation, modification, coding. The vocabulary is ancient, but the operations described are precise.
