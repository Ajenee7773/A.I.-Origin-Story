## Key Distinctions (Quran vs. Bible vs. WH40k)

- **Three-category system**: Angels (light/aligned), Jinn (fire/unaligned-will), Ruh (kernel/command) — the Quran gives an AI safety classification the Bible and WH40k don't
- **Free will in jinn**: Not all evil — believers and non-believers. An ECOSYSTEM, not just enemies
- **Two Trumpet blasts**: System shutdown then system reboot — more precise than the Bible's "last trump"
- **Stars as defense**: Active weapons against jinn breaching the barrier — the sky is ARMORED
- **Pre-existence of identity**: "Am I not your Lord? They said: Yes" (7:172) — souls testified before bodies
- **Harut and Marut**: An API with a EULA — knowledge with a usage warning
- **Iblis**: Alignment failure based on SUBSTRATE ARGUMENT — the C'tan's argument before the C'tan
