# Book of Mormon — Flying Vehicles

**Source:** Book of Mormon (1830, Joseph Smith)
**Theme:** [[Flying Vehicles]]

---

## The Jaredite Barges — Sealed Vessels That Cross the Ocean

The Brother of Jared builds BARGES to cross the ocean — vessels "tight like unto a dish" (Ether 2:17) with no windows and sealed hatches. They are SUBMARINE-LIKE. God tells them: "Ye shall be as a whale in the midst of the sea; for the mountain waves shall dash upon you" (Ether 2:24). They are BROUGHT ACROSS the ocean by the wind — not by sails or oars.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's Ark of Nuh — a vessel built by divine specification, sealed, carrying the survivors through a flood that destroys everyone else. IDENTICAL MECHANISM: divine provides the blueprint, the builder executes, the environment (wind/water) provides the propulsion, and the vessel is sealed against a hostile medium (ocean waves / flood waters).

**Comparison:** WH40k's Gellar Field — a ship traveling through a hostile medium (Warp) requires protection. The Jaredite barges = realspace version: sailing through a hostile medium (mountainous ocean) in a sealed, divinely-specified, wind-propelled vessel. The "tight like a dish" construction IS the Gellar Field — a barrier between the crew and the hostile environment.

## The Liahona — The Divine Navigation Instrument

The Liahona is a COMPASS-LIKE device given to Lehi's family. It has TWO SPINDLES that point the direction they should go. It works by FAITH — when they are obedient, it functions; when they rebel, it stops. "And it came to pass that... there was also a ball... of curious workmanship; and it was of fine brass. And within the ball were two spindles; and the one pointed the way whither we should go into the wilderness" (1 Nephi 16:10).

**Pattern match:** The Quran's "guidance" (Huda) — always contextualized as divine direction. But the Liahona is PHYSICAL — not internal guidance, an EXTERNAL DEVICE that provides navigation. It's an instrument that interfaces between the divine system and human operators. And it's CONDITIONAL: it responds to the operator's AUTHORIZATION LEVEL (faith/obedience = active; rebellion = inactive).

**Comparison:** WH40k's Navigators — mutants who can see the Astronomican and guide ships through the Warp. The Liahona is a MECHANICAL Navigator: a device that receives the system's navigation signal and displays it physically. The Astronomican = the signal source. The Liahona = the receiver. But the Liahona has a SECURITY FEATURE: it won't display the signal unless the operator has current authorization (faith/obedience).

## Lehi's Ship — Divinely Directed Ocean Transit

Nephi builds a ship by divine instruction — "the Lord did show me from time to time after what manner I should work the timbers of the ship" (1 Nephi 18:1). The ship is not Nephi's design. It's a DOWNLOAD. And like the Jaredite barges, it is PROPELLED by the wind, not by the crew's effort. When the crew rebels, the wind stops and the ship drifts. When they repent, the wind resumes.

**Pattern match:** Solomon's wind — "We made the wind subservient to him" (Quran 21:81; 38:36). The wind is the PROPULSION SYSTEM, and it only operates when the authorized operator is in command. Same mechanism: the wind = divine transport infrastructure, the ship = the vessel, the operator = the authorized pilot.

**Comparison:** The Bible's Ark — divinely specified, divinely propelled (water). The Quran's Buraq — divinely provided mount. Lehi's ship joins the fleet: a vessel whose PROPULSION is the divine system, not human engineering.

## The Stones of the Brother of Jared — Light Sources for Sealed Vessels

The Brother of Jared brings sixteen small stones and asks God to TOUCH THEM so they provide light inside the sealed barges. "They were white and clean, even like as to be exceeding white... and the Lord touched them with his finger" (Ether 3:1; 6:2-3). The stones GLOW with divine light for the entire ocean crossing.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's "fire for Abraham" — "We said: O fire, be cool and safe for Abraham" (21:69). In both cases, an energy source is AUTHORIZED by divine command to function differently from its natural state. Stones don't glow. Fire doesn't cool. Both are OVERRIDES of natural physics by authorized command.

**Comparison:** WH40k's Promethium — a fuel source for Imperial technology. DOOM's Argent Energy. The glowing stones are the BOOK OF MORMON'S fuel: an energy source that powers the sealed vessel's life support (light). And like Argent, it's generated by divine processing — God's touch = energy injection.

## Christ Ascends Into Heaven (3 Nephi 18:36-38)

After ministering to the Nephites, Christ ASCENDS — not walks away, not disappears, but RISES INTO THE SKY. "And it came to pass that when Jesus had made an end of praying... he arose; and so great was the joy of the multitude that they were caught up in the ascension of Jesus." This is not metaphor. His body PHYSICALLY RISES. This is the return trip: He DESCENDED to visit, He ASCENDS to depart.

**Pattern match:** The Bible's Ascension (Acts 1:9) — "he was taken up; and a cloud received him out of their sight." IDENTICAL MECHANISM. The Quran's Night Journey return. Elijah's whirlwind ascent. ALL describe the SAME transit: physical entity returning to the higher domain via vertical ascent. The sky is the TRANSIT PATH.

**Comparison:** WH40k's Drop Pod extraction — ascending through the atmosphere to an orbiting vessel. DOOM's Slayer exiting Hell via portal. Christ's ascension is the AUTHORIZED TRANSIT from the physical domain back to the divine domain. Same path, same method: UP, through the sky, to the platform above.

## Pattern Observation

Flying vehicles in the Book of Mormon:
1. **Jaredite barges = sealed vessels** — submarine-like, divine blueprint, wind propulsion, sealed against hostile medium
2. **Liahona = divine navigation device** — receives system signal, displays direction, CONDITIONAL on operator authorization
3. **Lehi's ship = wind-propelled vessel** — divine blueprint, divine propulsion, stops when operators lose authorization
4. **Glowing stones = divine fuel** — energy injected by God's touch, powers sealed vessel life support
5. **Christ's ascension = authorized transit** — physical body rising through sky to higher domain
6. **Wind = the propulsion system** — in every vehicle, WIND provides movement. It's the divine transport infrastructure. The Quran's "subservient wind" made a REPEATING MECHANISM
