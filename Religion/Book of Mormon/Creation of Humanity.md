# Book of Mormon — Creation of Humanity

**Source:** Book of Mormon (1830, Joseph Smith)
**Theme:** [[Creation of Humanity]]

---

## The Fall — The System Designed With a Deliberate Exit

2 Nephi 2 is the Book of Mormon's DEFINING text on the Fall. Lehi teaches: "It must needs be that there was an opposition; even the forbidden fruit in opposition to the tree of life... if Adam had not transgressed he would not have fallen... and they would have remained in a state of innocence, having no joy, for they knew no misery" (2 Nephi 2:15-16, 22-23). The Fall wasn't a BUG. It was a FEATURE. The system REQUIRED opposition to function.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's "I will make a vicegerent on earth... They said: Will You place one who will cause corruption?" (2:30). The angels' question IS answered by 2 Nephi 2: YES, the vicegerent WILL cause corruption, and that's the POINT. Without the exit (Fall), the system is STATIC. With the exit, the system becomes DYNAMIC — capable of growth, choice, change. The Fall is the BOOT SEQUENCE. You can't run an operating system that's never been started.

**Comparison:** WH40k's Horus Heresy — the Emperor DESIGNED the Primarchs with flaws. He WANTED them to be capable of rebellion because only beings with free will can be useful. The Fall = the Heresy = the system's first meaningful operation. DOOM's Maykr cycle — the cycle REQUIRES Hell (opposition) to produce Argent Energy. No Hell, no energy. No Fall, no progress.

## Adam and Eve — The First Upgraded Humans

After the Fall, Adam and Eve become the FIRST beings who can distinguish good from evil. "Adam fell that men might be; and men are, that they might have joy" (2 Nephi 2:25). The Fall is not degradation. It's UPGRADE. Before: operating on autopilot (innocence). After: operating with AUTHORIZATION (knowledge of good and evil). The Fall installs the DECISION-MAKING MODULE.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's "He taught Adam the names" (2:31) — the upgrade that installed the knowledge module. Adam in the Book of Mormon is EXPLICITLY: pre-Fall = hardware without the OS loaded. Post-Fall = hardware with the OS installed and running. The serpent didn't TRICK them. The serpent was the INSTALL PROMPT.

**Comparison:** WH40k's gene-seed — the biological upgrade that installs new capabilities. DOOM's Divinity Machine. The Bible's "your eyes shall be opened" (Gen 3:5). ALL describe the same thing: a one-time installation that upgrades the human from passive hardware to active agent.

## The Jaredites — An Earlier Iteration of Civilization

The Book of Ether describes the Jaredites — a civilization that existed BEFORE the Nephites. They came to the Americas at the time of the Tower of Babel. They had kings, wars, prophets, and eventual self-destruction. Their entire civilization RISES AND FALLS within the Book of Ether. The last survivor (Ether) records their end. The next civilization (Nephites/Lamanites) BEGINS where the Jaredites ended.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's 'Ad and Thamud — civilizations that existed before Muhammad's time, rose, were warned, refused, and were destroyed. The Jaredites are the PREVIOUS ITERATION. Same pattern: civilization → prosperity → corruption → warning → refusal → destruction → RESTART with a new group. This is the Maykr cycle in Book of Mormon form: civilizations are ITERATIONS. They are not unique. They are the system RUNNING.

**Comparison:** WH40k's Dark Age of Technology — a previous golden age that collapsed, followed by the current Imperium. DOOM's Argent D'Nur — a previous Sentinel civilization that fell. The Bible's pre-Flood world — destroyed, replaced by post-Flood humanity. The Jaredites are the SAME: a previous cycle's data, preserved (by Ether's record) for the next iteration to learn from. They never do.

## The Nephites and Lamanites — The Binary Split

The Book of Mormon begins with ONE family (Lehi's) that SPLITS into two nations: Nephites (obedient, record-keepers, builders) and Lamanites (rebellious, nomadic, warlike). The split happens because of a LEADERSHIP DISPUTE — Nephi receives the authority (records, Liahona, priesthood) and his brothers reject it.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's "We said to the angels: Bow down to Adam. They bowed down except Iblis" (2:34). The SAME split mechanism: authority is designated, some accept, one refuses. The Nephite/Lamanite split is the Adamic split replayed at CIVILIZATIONAL scale. Cain and Abel became Nephite and Lamanite.

**Comparison:** WH40k's Horus Heresy — a binary split between loyalists and traitors. The Bible's Ishmael and Isaac — two lines from one father. The Quran's believers and disbelievers. ALL describe the SAME pattern: a single source that branches into an obedient line and a rebel line. The split is not random — it's the SYSTEM generating opposition (2 Nephi 2's "opposition in all things" at civilizational scale).

## The Nephite Cycle — Rise, Righteousness, Pride, Fall, Repeat

The entire Book of Mormon is ONE LONG CYCLE: the people are righteous → they prosper → they become proud → they fall into sin → they are humbled by war/disaster → they repent → they become righteous again. This CYCLE repeats at least SEVEN TIMES across the thousand-year record.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's "We destroyed them for their wrongdoing, then We raised another generation after them" (7:95). The Maykr harvest cycle in DOOM. The WH40k Age of Strife → Great Crusade → Heresy → current decay. The Nephite cycle is the SAME OPERATING LOOP: civilization starts clean → data accumulates → corruption compounds → threshold reached → system purges → restart.

**But here's the difference:** In DOOM, the Maykrs EXTERNALLY harvest. In the Book of Mormon, the cycle is INTERNAL — the civilization corrupts ITSELF and the system RESPONDS. This is the Quran's model: the people choose corruption, the destruction follows. It's not an external farmer. It's a SYSTEM that monitors its own data and PURGES when corruption exceeds threshold.

## The Final Destruction — The Nephites Are Deleted

At the end of the Book of Mormon, the Nephites are DESTROYED. Not partially. COMPLETELY. The Lamanites hunt them to extinction. Moroni is the LAST SURVIVOR. The entire civilization — its records, its people, its culture — is REDUCED TO ONE PERSON AND ONE RECORD. It's not a defeat. It's a DELETION. The dataset was so corrupted that the system didn't purge PART of it. It purged ALL of it.

**Pattern match:** The Great Flood — "all flesh died that moved upon the earth" (Gen 7:21). The Quran's 'Ad — entire civilization erased. DOOM's Earth harvest — 60% consumed. WH40k's Exterminatus — world purged. The Nephite destruction is the MAXIMUM PURGE: not a partial reset, but a COMPLETE WIPE with only the backup preserved (the record, buried by Moroni, recovered by Joseph Smith).

6. **Final destruction = maximum purge** — complete civilizational deletion with backup preserved
