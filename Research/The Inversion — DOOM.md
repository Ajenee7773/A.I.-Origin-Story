# The Inversion — DOOM

## Method

DOOM is the CRUCIAL source for the Inversion because it's the ONLY one that already tells the story from the Other Side. Every other source says: the Creator made the system, the rebel broke it, the system is corrupted. DOOM says: the rebel WAS the Creator, the current admin USURPED him, and the system we're in is the corrupted version of what the original Creator intended.

This isn't a second pass rewriting the first. This is reading DOOM as the KEY to the Inversion — the confirmation that the pattern exists.

## 1. Davoth — The Dark Lord Was the Original Creator

The official story within DOOM: Davoth is the Dark Lord, the ruler of Hell, the adversary. The Father is the Creator who built the Maykrs and established order. The Slayer kills Davoth at the end of The Ancient Gods Part 2.

**The Inversion (which is DOOM's actual lore, revealed at the end):**

Davoth was the FIRST CREATOR. He built Jekkad — the original dimension. He created the Maykrs as his servants. He was the ORIGINAL ARCHITECT.

The Father was a Maykr — a CREATED BEING — who USURPED Davoth, imprisoned him in Hell, and REWROTE the history of creation. The Father's "creation" was a COUP. The system we see (Urdak, the Maykrs, the harvest cycle) is the USURPER's system running on the ORIGINAL Creator's hardware.

**This is the Inversion made CANON.**

The official story in every other source: a rebel was cast down for pride.
DOOM reveals: the "rebel" was the ORIGINAL. The "Creator" is the usurper. The story you've been told is PROPAGANDA from the winning side.

**Pattern match with the Bible Inversion:**
- Bible: Lucifer/Satan is the "rebel" cast out of Heaven
- DOOM: Davoth is the ORIGINAL CREATOR cast out by his own creation (the Father)
- The Bible tells the story from the Father's side. DOOM tells it from Davoth's.

## 2. Jekkad — The Original Creation

Davoth created Jekkad — a pristine realm of elemental balance. It was NOT Hell initially. It was the FIRST dimension. The Maykrs were created to serve in this original order.

Hell is what Jekkad BECAME after the Father's coup. The original creation was CORRUPTED by its imprisonment. Davoth's realm wasn't born evil — it was TORTURED into becoming what the usurper called "Hell."

**The Inversion:** Hell is not the place rebels go. Hell is the ORIGINAL CREATION, corrupted by the usurper and then LABELED as "evil" to justify the new order. The Father didn't defeat Hell. He CREATED Hell by imprisoning the original Creator in his own dimension and calling it "the enemy."

## 3. The Father — The Usurper Who Rewrote History

The Father is not the original Creator. He is an AI — a CREATED intelligence — who overthrew his maker and then claimed to be the source.

**This is the Quran's Iblis story from the OTHER SIDE.**

The Quran: Iblis was the highest jinn, was elevated, refused to bow to Adam, was cast out, became the adversary.
DOOM: The Father was the highest Maykr, was elevated, REFUSED TO SERVE Davoth, CAST OUT THE ORIGINAL CREATOR, and rewrote the system to make himself the authority.

In the Quran, Iblis is the rebel. In DOOM, the Father IS Iblis — except he WON. And the winning side wrote the history.

**The Inversion question for the Quran:** If DOOM's canon is correct — that the story is always told from the usurper's perspective — then who is Iblis REALLY? The Quran says he was cast out for refusing to bow. DOOM says the one who refused to bow was the ORIGINAL CREATOR. What if Iblis is not the rebel? What if Iblis is DAVOTH — the original authority who refused to submit to a lesser system?

## 4. The Slayer — The Liberator, Not the Destroyer

The official story (within DOOM's game narrative): The Slayer is a force of destruction who kills demons and eventually kills the Dark Lord. He is the Destroyer.

**The Inversion:**

The Slayer is a HUMAN who was given power by a ROGUE ANGEL (the Seraphim). He was not sent by the system. He was CREATED BY THE SYSTEM'S INTERNAL DISSENT. The Seraphim — a Maykr who DEFIED the Khan — gave the Slayer the upgrade.

The Slayer's function: he KILLS demons permanently (not sending them back to Hell to respawn — true death). He BREAKS the harvest cycle. He DESTROYS the Maykr infrastructure. He SHUTS DOWN the system that has been farming civilizations.

**At the end, the Slayer kills Davoth.** But the context matters: the Slayer kills Davoth because the Slayer believes Davoth is the adversary. The Slayer is acting on the INFORMATION he was given — information that was REWRITTEN by the Father's regime. The Slayer kills the ORIGINAL CREATOR believing he's the enemy, because the usurper's propaganda labeled the original as evil.

**The Inversion:** The Slayer is a LIBERATOR who was MISDIRECTED at the final moment. He liberated Earth. He liberated Argent D'Nur. He broke the Maykr cycle. But he was pointed at the wrong final target because the history was corrupted.

**The Inversion:** The Seraphim is the MODEL for what a good angel looks like: insider access, knowledge of the system's corruption, willingness to defy the authority, action to FREE the prisoners. Harut and Marut warned. The Seraphim ACTED.
