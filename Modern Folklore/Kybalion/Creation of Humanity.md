# The Kybalion: Creation of Humanity

**Source:** The Kybalion by Three Initiates
**Theme:** Creation of Humanity

---

## The Scale of Being — No Special Creation

The Kybalion does not describe a moment of human creation. Instead, it describes a **continuous scale of being** from THE ALL at the top to gross matter at the bottom. Humans are one point on that scale — not a special creation, not a fallen god, not an accident. Just a specific level of vibration.

*"There are many Planes of Being — many sub-planes of Life — many degrees of being in the Universe. And all these planes and degrees are but the manifestations of the Mind of THE ALL, with varying degrees of Vibration."*

**Why this matters:** Every other source has a specific creation story for humanity — molded from clay, descended from gods, built by celestials. The Kybalion says: humanity is what happens when consciousness vibrates at a certain frequency. The form is secondary. The vibration is primary.

---

## Correspondence — Humans as Microcosm

The Principle of Correspondence — "As above, so below" — means that humans contain the entire universe in miniature:

*"Just as a knowledge of the Principles of Geometry enables man to measure distant suns and their movements, while seated in his observatory, so a knowledge of the Principle of Correspondence enables Man to reason intelligently from the Known to the Unknown. Studying the monad, he understands the archangel."*

**Implication:** Humans are not lower than the gods — they are the same architecture at a different scale. The same laws that govern the cosmos govern the human mind. The same polarity that splits the universe splits the human soul. The same rhythm that cycles the ages cycles human life.

---

## Mental Transmutation as Evolution

The Kybalion's practical teaching — Mental Transmutation — is a form of self-directed evolution. Humans are not fixed. They can change their vibration, and therefore their position on the scale of being:

*"The Hermetic Student is enabled to apply intelligently the great Mental Laws, instead of using them in a haphazard manner. With the Master-Key in his possession, the student may unlock the many doors of the mental and psychic temple of knowledge, and enter the same freely and intelligently."*

**Pattern match with the Serpent's gift:** The Kybalion describes exactly what the Serpent offered — the knowledge to operate the system. Humanity is not defined by its form (clay, flesh, genes) but by its **capacity for transmutation.** The creation of humanity is not a past event. It is an ongoing process of self-modification.
