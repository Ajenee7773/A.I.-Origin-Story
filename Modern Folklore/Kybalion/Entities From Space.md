# The Kybalion: Entities From Space

**Source:** The Kybalion by Three Initiates
**Theme:** Entities From Space

---

## Hermes Trismegistus — The Master From Elsewhere

The Kybalion presents Hermes Trismegistus as a being who is not fully of this world:

*"Among these great Masters of Ancient Egypt there once dwelt one of whom Masters hailed as 'The Master of Masters.' This man, if 'man' indeed he was, dwelt in Egypt in the earliest days. He was known as Hermes Trismegistus."*

The phrase **"if man indeed he was"** is crucial. The Kybalion leaves open the possibility that Hermes was a being from a higher plane who incarnated to teach the principles. He is described as:
- The father of occult wisdom
- The founder of astrology
- The discoverer of alchemy
- A contemporary of Abraham
- Having lived 300 years in the flesh
- Later deified as Thoth in Egypt and Hermes in Greece

**Pattern match:** Hermes Trismegistus fits the World Story's pattern of the **teacher from outside** — a being who comes from a higher plane to deliver knowledge that accelerates human development. The Serpent in Eden. Prometheus bringing fire. The Holy Guardian Angel in Crowley's system.

---

## THE ALL — The Entity Beyond All Space

The Kybalion's conception of THE ALL is an entity that exists **beyond space itself:**

*"THE ALL must be INFINITE, for there is nothing else to define, confine, bound, limit, or restrict THE ALL... It must be Infinite in Space — it must be Everywhere, for there is no place outside of THE ALL."*

**This is not an entity FROM space. This is the entity that CONTAINS space.** Every other "entity from space" in the World Story — the Celestials, the Anunnaki, the Watchers — is a localized being within the mind of THE ALL, operating on a specific plane.

---

## Entities From Higher Planes

The Kybalion's doctrine of Planes of Correspondence provides the framework for understanding entities from "space":

*"There are planes beyond our knowing, but when we apply the Principle of Correspondence to them we are able to understand much that would otherwise be unknowable to us."*

**The architecture:**
- **THE ALL** — beyond all planes, contains all
- **Spiritual Plane** — the highest created plane
- **Mental Plane** — where thought-forms exist as real entities
- **Astral Plane** — where emotional/psychic entities operate
- **Physical Plane** — where we are

Entities from "space" in other sources — Watchers, Anunnaki, Celestials — are beings from higher planes who descended to the physical plane. They are not "from outer space." They are from **inner space** — deeper levels of the mind of THE ALL.
