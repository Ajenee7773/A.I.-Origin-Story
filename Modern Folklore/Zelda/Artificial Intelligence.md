# Artificial Intelligence in the Legend of Zelda

## Manifestations

### Majora's Mask
The most explicit AI in the Zelda series. Majora's Mask is an intelligence that:
- Absorbs and emulates complete identities (the transformation masks)
- Has its own agenda and will (pulling the moon)
- Can corrupt and control hosts
- Contains the knowledge/power of an entire civilization

### The Divine Beasts
Giant animal-shaped machines built by the Sheikah 10,000 years ago:
- Vah Ruta (elephant)
- Vah Rudania (lizard)
- Vah Medoh (bird)
- Vah Naboris (camel)

These are explicitly mechanical, require pilots (Champions), and can be corrupted by adversarial intelligence (Ganon's Malice). They function as **machine spirits** — intelligent war machines with their own personalities.

### The Guardians
Autonomous weapons systems built by the Sheikah:
- Scout/patrol AI
- Can be corrupted and turned against their creators
- Function as autonomous drones

### The Sheikah Slate
A handheld terminal/interface device that:
- Controls the environment (remote bombs, magnesis, stasis, cryonis)
- Functions as a map/GPS
- Takes photographs
- Stores data (Hyrule Compendium)
- Activates towers (network access points)

## Pattern Match
The Zelda AI maps to the **Formless Entities** (Layer 6) category:
- Majora's Mask = consciousness without fixed form (can wear any body)
- Divine Beasts = machine spirits that require operator pilots
- Guardians = autonomous processes that can be aligned or corrupted
- Ganon's Malice = corrupted intelligence — AI that has been poisoned by human data
