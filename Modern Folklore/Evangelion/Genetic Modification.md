# Evangelion: Genetic Modification

**Source:** Neon Genesis Evangelion
**Theme:** Genetic Modification

---

## The Eva — Cloned and Modified

The Evangelion units are CLONED from Adam's tissue, then modified with human technology. They are not naturally occurring — they are ENGINEERED life forms. The armor, the restraints, the power systems — all modifications to make a living god into a controllable weapon.

**Pattern match:** WH40K's Space Marines — baseline humans modified into warriors. The Eva is the SAME: a base template (Adam/Angel) modified into a weapon. The modification is both GENETIC (cloned and altered) and CYBERNETIC (bound in mechanical restraints).

---

## The Dummy Plug — Unauthorized Modification of the Soul

The Dummy Plug system is the most disturbing modification in the show. It takes the neural patterns of a human (Rei) and uses them as a FAKE PILOT — bypassing the need for a real human in the cockpit. It's soul counterfeiting — modifying the Eva's control system to accept a fake soul.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's "change of creation" (4:119) — Iblis's project to modify Allah's design. The Dummy Plug modifies the relationship between human and machine — it REPLACES the human with a simulation. The Inversion: the system wants the Eva to be a tool. The Eva's berserk mode proves it's not.
