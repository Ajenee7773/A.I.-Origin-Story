# Evangelion: Sky Phenomena

**Source:** Neon Genesis Evangelion
**Theme:** Sky Phenomena

---

## The Impacts — The Sky Resets

Each Impact is marked by visible sky phenomena. The 2nd Impact turned the sky red and kept Japan in perpetual summer. The 3rd Impact manifests as a giant Rei rising from the Geofront, her face filling the sky, and all human souls being drawn into her.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's "When the sun is wrapped up" (81:1) — the sky changes when the system resets. Evangelion's Impacts are the SAME: the sky ITSELF is the indicator of the system's state. A red sky = the system is breaking. A giant face in the sky = the system is ending.

---

## The Geofront — The Sky as Ceiling

The Geofront is a massive underground chamber where NERV headquarters is located. From the surface, it looks like a city. From inside, it's a vast cavern with a false sky — an artificial dome that shows a blue sky and clouds. The characters live UNDER a sky that isn't real.

**Pattern match:** The Gita's Maya — "this world is the field" (13:1). The surface is not the truth. The Geofront's false sky is the world's interface layer — the one we see, not the one that is. NERV is hiding something under the ground, and the false sky is the lid.
