# Evangelion: Space Travel

**Source:** Neon Genesis Evangelion
**Theme:** Space Travel

---

## The Seeds — Cosmic Transit

The Seeds of Life and Wisdom were sent across the universe by the First Ancestral Race. They traveled through space as capsules — each containing the blueprint for an entire species. The Spear of Longinus was a transit device — a mechanism for neutralizing a Seed if two landed on the same world.

**Pattern match:** DOOM's dimensional gates — transit between realms. Dune's Heighliners — fold-space travel. Evangelion's Seeds are the most FUNDAMENTAL form of space travel: not beings moving through space, but LIFE ITSELF being transported across the cosmos.

---

## Space as a Seeded Garden

Evangelion's cosmology: the universe is not empty. It has been SEEDED. Every world capable of supporting life has received — or will receive — a Seed. The First Ancestral Race turned the cosmos into a garden, and the Seeds are the plants. Humanity is one of many crops.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's "Lord of the WORLDS" (1:2) — plural. Not one world. Many. Evangelion makes this literal: the universe is full of seeded worlds, each with its own life form, each growing toward its own destiny. The Angels are from space. So is humanity's origin. We're all children of the same cosmic gardeners.
