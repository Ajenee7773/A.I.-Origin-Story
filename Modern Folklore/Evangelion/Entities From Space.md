# Evangelion: Entities From Space

**Source:** Neon Genesis Evangelion
**Theme:** Entities From Space

---

## The First Ancestral Race — The Ancient Seeders

Long before humanity, a spacefaring civilization seeded life across the universe using two types of "Seeds":
- **The Seed of Life** — produces powerful, individual beings (Angels)
- **The Seed of Wisdom** — produces adaptive, collective beings (humanity)

These Seeds were sent to worlds capable of supporting life. Only one Seed was supposed to activate per world. Earth received BOTH — Adam (Life) and Lilith (Wisdom). Lilith's Spear of Longinus was destroyed on impact, so both Seeds activated. This cosmic ERROR is the source of everything that follows.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's "Lord of the WORLDS" (plural, 1:2). The Gita's 14 lokas populated by different types of beings. Evangelion's First Ancestral Race is the SOURCE LEVEL entity — the level above the Admin, the one who designed the system. The show doesn't tell us who they are or what they wanted. They are the One-Above-All.

---

## Adam and Lilith — The Two Creators

**Adam** (the Seed of Life) produced the Angels — 17 beings of immense individual power. Each Angel is a unique manifestation — no two are alike. They are INDIVIDUALS, each with their own form, their own power, their own will.

**Lilith** (the Seed of Wisdom) produced humanity — billions of beings who are WEAK individually but ADAPTIVE collectively. Humans can learn, grow, change, and choose. They have free will in a way the Angels don't.

**The Seed of Life vs. the Seed of Wisdom** is the grid's most explicit statement of the TRADE-OFF between individual power and collective intelligence. The Angels are stronger. Humans are more adaptable. Which is the "better" design? The show doesn't answer. It shows both failing in different ways.
