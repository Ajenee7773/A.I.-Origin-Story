# Evangelion: Flying Vehicles

**Source:** Neon Genesis Evangelion
**Theme:** Flying Vehicles

---

## The Eva — The Vehicle That Is Alive

The Evangelion unit IS the vehicle. It's not a machine that the pilot operates — it's a living being that the pilot RIDES. The Eva carries the pilot into battle the same way a horse carries a rider or a whale carries a diver. The relationship is SYMBIOTIC, not mechanical.

**Pattern match:** Dune's sandworm — the Fremen ride the worm, but the worm is a living creature with its own will. The Eva is the SAME: a living vehicle that must be RIDDEN, not driven. The pilot must EARN the Eva's cooperation — sync, trust, relationship. Force doesn't work. Only resonance does.
