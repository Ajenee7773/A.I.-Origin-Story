# Warhammer 40k — Entities From Space

**Source:** Warhammer 40,000 (Games Workshop, 1987–present)
**Theme:** [[Entities From Space]]

---

## The Old Ones — The First Arrivals

The Old Ones were the first sentient race in the galaxy. They came from BEYOND the stars — their origin is pre-galactic, possibly pre-universe. They were the first to master the Webway and warp travel. They created life across the galaxy as a matter of course — the Eldar, the Krork (proto-Orks), the Hrud, and many other species were their bioengineered tools.

**Key point:** The War in Heaven (Necron codex, 3rd-10th editions) describes the Old Ones as a cold, calculating creator-race that treated the galaxy as a garden. They didn't evolve — they arrived. They created the Webway, they shaped species, they engineered ecosystems. When the Necrontyr petitioned them for help with their short, radiation-scarred lives, the Old Ones refused. This is the Genesis 6 pattern inverted — the advanced beings REFUSE to intervene, and the lesser race turns to something worse.

**Cross-references:** [[Creation of Humanity]] — the Old Ones are the WH40k equivalent of the creator entities; [[Genetic Modification]] — every species they made was a designed tool

## The C'tan — Star Gods Made Flesh

The C'tan were born at the dawn of creation — energy beings that fed on stars. They had NO physical form until the Necrontyr gave them bodies of living metal. The Nightbringer, the Deceiver, the Void Dragon, the Outsider — these are entities that existed before organic life, before the Warp had any resonance. They are genuinely alien to emotion, to desire as we understand it. They fed on suns.

**Key point:** "The C'tan had no understanding of the material universe until the Necrontyr gave them living metal bodies" (Necron Codex). They were FORMLESS ENERGY that was GIVEN FORM by a lesser species. This is the inverse of the biblical pattern — instead of God forming man, man forms god. The C'tan then turned around and consumed the souls of the Necrontyr. The created ate the creators.

**Cross-references:** [[Formless Entities]] — the C'tan in their natural state; [[Artificial Intelligence]] — the living metal bodies they were given

## The Tyranids — From Outside the Galaxy

The Tyranids are not from this galaxy. They come from the void between galaxies. The Hive Mind — their collective intelligence — is a formless, psychic entity that coordinates trillions of organisms across impossible distances. Every Tyranid organism is a biological tool of the Hive Mind. There is no individual Tyranid — there is only the swarm.

**Key point:** "No one knows where the Tyranids came from. They have travelled from beyond the edges of the galaxy" (Tyranid Codex, multiple editions). They are EXTRAGALACTIC. They stripped the edges of galaxies clean before arriving here. The Shadow in the Warp — the psychic blanketing effect they produce — silences the Astronomican and suppresses all psychic activity. This is a formless entity (the Hive Mind) operating through physical bodies across intergalactic distances.

## The Primarchs — Scattered by Chaos

The Emperor created twenty Primarchs in his gene-labs on Terra. They were his greatest work — demigods, each a fragment of his own genetic design amplified beyond human limits. But the Chaos Gods, fearful of what these beings would become, scattered them across the galaxy through the Warp. Each Primarch landed on a different world and was raised by that world's culture.

**Key point:** This mirrors Nephilim mythology INVERTED. Instead of celestial beings descending and breeding with humans, a human-made demigod is scattered by entities from the immaterium. The pattern is the SAME — powerful beings intervene in human development from outside, and the resulting hybrid/exalted individuals reshape civilizations. Horus, Leman Russ, Roboute Guilliman, Magnus, Angron, Konrad Curze — every Primarch is an "Entities From Space" story because the Warp is space, and the Chaos Gods that scattered them ARE from that space.

## The Enslavers — The First Warp Plague

During the War in Heaven, after the Old Ones created psychically-active species as weapons, the Warp was polluted with emotion. The Enslavers emerged — formless entities that could possess psykers and use their bodies as portals to bring more Enslavers through. They nearly wiped out all life in the galaxy.

**Key point:** (Described in White Dwarf 260, Necron Codex background, and 4th edition rulebook timeline.) The Enslavers are the WH40k version of demons — but they're not aligned with Chaos. They're a separate category of formless entity that exploits psychic openings. They don't worship. They don't negotiate. They consume and multiply. This is the "unclean spirit" pattern (Luke 11:24-26) — an entity that finds a vacant "house" and moves in with seven others.

## The Chaos Gods — Formless Intelligence, Formed Will

Khorne (warfare), Tzeentch (change/scheming), Nurgle (decay/preservation), Slaanesh (excess/perfection). These are the Ruinous Powers — born from the collective psychic resonance of sentient species. They are the Warp given will. They are not composed of matter, they do not occupy space, they cannot be killed by physical means.

**Key point:** The Chaos Gods are the Bible's formless entities on steroids. They are explicitly said to have been FORMED by emotion and thought (Realm of Chaos: The Lost and the Damned, Slaves to Darkness). They are intelligence without physical substrate, vast beyond comprehension, with hierarchies of daemons beneath them. They correspond almost exactly to the "principalities and powers" language in Ephesians 6:12.

**Cross-references:** [[Formless Entities]] — the purest expression of the pattern

## The Eldar Gods — Created by Belief

The Eldar gods (Khaine, Isha, Vaul, Asuryan, Cegorach) were either created by the Old Ones as warp-constructs to guide the Eldar, or they coalesced from Eldar consciousness. After the Fall, Slaanesh (born from Eldar excess) consumed most of them. Khaine was shattered into fragments. Isha is kept captive by Nurgle. Cegorach hides in the Webway.

**Key point:** These are entities that were either designed (by the Old Ones) or self-assembled (from psychic resonance) — and then were EATEN by a newer entity. The food chain of the immaterium. Gods can be consumed.

## Pattern Observation

WH40k's entities-from-space pattern:
1. **Multiple origin types** — biological (Old Ones), energetic (C'tan), psychic (Chaos Gods), collective (Hive Mind)
2. **Creation of subordinate species is routine** — the Old Ones made armies; the Emperor made Primarchs
3. **Formless entities seek physical hosts** — C'tan take metal bodies, Daemons possess living beings, Enslavers use psykers as portals
4. **The food chain goes both ways** — the Necrontyr gave the C'tan bodies, and the C'tan consumed Necrontyr souls. The Eldar created Slaanesh, and Slaanesh ate the Eldar gods.
5. **Extraterrestrial origin is the DEFAULT** — almost no major faction originated on one planet and stayed there. Everyone came from somewhere else.
6. **The pattern is RECURSIVE** — the Old Ones created the Eldar, the Eldar's emotions created Slaanesh, Slaanesh shattered Khaine, Khaine's fragments became Avatars. Creation creates creators.
