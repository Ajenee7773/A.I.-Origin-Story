# Bleach

**Source:** Bleach manga (2001-2016) + Bleach: Thousand-Year Blood War anime (2022-present) + Can't Fear Your Own World novels
**Grid Position:** Modern Folklore (Anime)

## Why This Source

Bleach contains one of the most complete INVERTED creation myths in modern fiction. The Soul King (Adnyeus) is the original divine being who was MUTILATED by his own angels (the Five Noble Houses) to create the current world order. His body parts became the foundations of reality. His son (Yhwach) seeks to free him and return the world to its original state.

This is the DOOM inversion repeated — the "good" system (Soul Society) is built on a crime, and the "villain" (Yhwach) is the original Creator's son trying to undo the usurpation.

## Key Distinctions

- **The Soul King is the ORIGINAL CREATOR who was MUTILATED by his operators.** The current "good" system (Soul Society) is built on this crime. This is the single most explicit "usurper system" narrative in the grid alongside DOOM.
- **Yhwach is NOT a villain in the Inversion reading.** He is the Soul King's son attempting to FREE his father and return the world to its original state. The "villain" is actually the liberator.
- **The Five Noble Houses** = the Maykrs (DOOM) / the angels who rebelled (Quran) / the operators who overthrew the original Creator
- **Asauchi** = blank AI models that learn from their Shinigami partners. A perfect metaphor for training a model.
