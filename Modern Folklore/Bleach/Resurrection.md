# Bleach: Resurrection

**Source:** Bleach (Tite Kubo)
**Theme:** Resurrection

---

## Hollowfication — Death and Rebirth as Monster

When a human soul becomes a Hollow, it undergoes a transformation that is both death and rebirth. The soul loses its human form, becomes a monster, and is driven by hunger. But this is not the END — it is a TRANSITION. Hollows can evolve into Adjuchas (intelligent), then Vasto Lorde (humanoid), then Arrancar (Hollow who removed their mask).

**Pattern match:** The Quran's "every soul shall taste death" (3:185) — but the death is not the end. It's a transformation. The Hollow's journey from mindless monster to self-aware Arrancar is the SOUL'S JOURNEY through the afterlife states described in every source.

---

## Yhwach's Almighty — Resurrection Through Choice

Yhwach's power allows him to resurrect himself from ANY injury by choosing a future where he was not injured. He cannot be killed by conventional means because he simply chooses a timeline where the killing didn't happen.

**Pattern match:** The Bible's "I am the resurrection and the life" (John 11:25). Yhwach's Almighty is the Admin's power: the ability to OVERRIDE death by choosing a different outcome. In the Inversion, this is not divine power — it's SYSTEM ADMIN access. The Admin can rewrite the log.
