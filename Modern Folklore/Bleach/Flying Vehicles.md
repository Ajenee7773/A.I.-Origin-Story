# Bleach: Flying Vehicles

**Source:** Bleach (Tite Kubo)
**Theme:** Flying Vehicles

---

## Senkaimon — Realm Gates

Shinigami travel between realms through Senkaimon — gates that open between the World of the Living and Soul Society. A Senkaimon is a portal that appears as a sliding door in mid-air, opened by a specific key. The Jigokucho (Hell Butterflies) guide Shinigami through the Dangai (the space between worlds).

**Pattern match:** The Quran's Bifrost-analogue — a managed gateway between domains. The Senkaimon is exactly like Heimdall's Bifrost in Norse/Marvel cosmology: a controlled transit point between realms.

---

## Garganta — Hollow Transit

Hollows travel through Garganta — they tear open the fabric of reality itself to move between Hueco Mundo and the other realms. Unlike the Senkaimon (which uses a gate), Garganta is a FORCIBLE breach of the barrier between worlds.

**Pattern match:** WH40K's Warp breaches — the barrier between Materium and Warp being torn open. Garganta is Hell breaking through: unauthorized transit by formless entities.
