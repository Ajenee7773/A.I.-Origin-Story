# Bleach: Sky Phenomena

**Source:** Bleach (Tite Kubo)
**Theme:** Sky Phenomena

---

## The Three Realms — The Architecture of the Sky

Bleach's cosmology has three primary realms stacked vertically:
1. **Soul Society** (above) — the realm of souls, heaven-analogue
2. **World of the Living** (middle) — the physical earth
3. **Hueco Mundo** (below) — the hollow realm, hell-analogue

Between them runs the **Dangai** (the Precipice World) — a dimension of shifting time and space that connects the realms. Hell exists as a FOURTH realm, sealed beneath everything.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's 7 heavens. The Gita's 14 lokas. The Bible's 3-tier cosmology (heaven, earth, underworld). Bleach's three realms + Dangai + Hell = the SAME tiered architecture, drawn as a stack of parallel dimensions instead of a vertical hierarchy.

---

## The Soul King Palace — The Central Pillar

Above all three realms floats the Soul King Palace — the domain of the Soul King and the Royal Guard (Zero Division). It is accessible only through specific methods (the Oken, or the Royal Guard's own transport).

**Pattern match:** The Quran's "He established Himself above the Throne" (7:54). The Soul King Palace is the Throne — the center of the system where the Administrator resides. But in the Inversion, the Soul King isn't ruling from the Throne. He's IMPRISONED there.
