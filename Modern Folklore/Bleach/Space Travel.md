# Bleach: Space Travel

**Source:** Bleach (Tite Kubo)
**Theme:** Space Travel

---

## The Dangai — The Precipice World

The Dangai is the dimension BETWEEN the realms. It has its own physics — time flows differently, the environment is hostile, and it requires a guide (the Jigokucho) to navigate safely. It is the BOUNDARY LAYER between dimensional domains.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's Barzakh — "a barrier between them they do not cross" (55:19-20). The Dangai IS the Barzakh: the partition between worlds that must be crossed through authorized transit. The Gita's space between lokas. The Dangai is the transit corridor — the network through which all inter-realm travel flows.

---

## The Four Realms

Bleach's full cosmology: Soul Society (heaven), World of the Living (earth), Hueco Mundo (hell/void), Hell (the pit). Four realms, each with different physics, different entities, different rules. Connected by the Dangai network.

**Pattern match:** Marvel's Nine Worlds + the Negative Zone + the Microverse. The Gita's 14 lokas. The Bible's heaven/earth/sheol. Bleach's four-realm model is the MOST FUNCTIONAL description — these aren't just levels, they're DIMENSIONS with specific access protocols.
