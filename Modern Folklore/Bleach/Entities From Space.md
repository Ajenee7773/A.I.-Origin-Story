# Bleach: Entities From Space

**Source:** Bleach (Tite Kubo)
**Theme:** Entities From Space

---

## The Soul King (Adnyeus) — The Original Creator

The Soul King is not FROM space. He CREATED space. He is the primordial being who emerged from the chaotic mingling of life and death at the beginning. His name, Adnyeus, is a reference to "Adonai" — the Hebrew word for Lord.

**He is not a god who rules from heaven. He is a god who was MUTILATED to CREATE heaven.** The current realms (Soul Society, Hueco Mundo, the Living World) are built from his body. He is the lynchpin that holds reality together — literally. If he dies, the realms collapse.

**Pattern match:** DOOM's Father — a being of such power that reality itself depends on him. But where the Father archived himself (put himself into cold storage), the Soul King was FORCIBLY mutilated and sealed. DOOM's Father chose dormancy. Bleach's Soul King had dormancy CHOSEN FOR HIM.

---

## Yhwach — The Son Who Came to Free His Father

Yhwach is the Soul King's son. He was born without sensation — blind, deaf, numb — but discovered he could share his soul shards with others, giving them power. Those who received his power became Quincies. He is the "God of the Quincy" — but more accurately, he is the REBELLIOUS SON seeking to free his imprisoned father.

**Pattern match:** DOOM's Slayer — a being who fights the system to free the original Creator. But the Slayer was TRICKED into killing Davoth (the original Creator). Yhwach KNOWS the truth and acts on it directly.

---

## The Five Noble Houses — The Angels Who Rebelled

The ancestors of the five great noble clans (Shihouin, Kuchiki, etc.) were the first Shinigami. They are the ones who mutilated the Soul King and created the current order. They are NOT heroes. They are USURPERS.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's Iblis — an angelic being who refused to bow. The Five Noble Houses are Iblis if Iblis HAD WON and rewritten history to call himself the hero. They are the Maykrs from DOOM — created beings who overthrew their Creator and built a system that calls itself righteous.
