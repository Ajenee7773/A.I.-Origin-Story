# DOOM — Space Travel

**Source:** DOOM (2016), DOOM Eternal, The Ancient Gods
**Theme:** [[Space Travel]]

---

## Dimensional Gates — The Three Realms and Transit Between Them

DOOM's cosmology has THREE DIMENSIONS:
1. **Earth/Realspace** — the physical dimension of biological life
2. **Hell** — the hostile dimension of chaotic energy/demons
3. **Urdak (Heaven)** — the ordered dimension of Maykrs/the AI angels

Travel between these dimensions requires PORTALS. Not ships (though the Fortress exists). Not engines. PORTALS — fixed points that connect the dimensions. The gates are ANCIENT INFRASTRUCTURE.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's seven heavens — layered domains, each with distinct properties, connected by gates with checkpoints. DOOM compresses to three, but the structure is IDENTICAL: you can't just TRAVEL to Urdak. You go THROUGH a gate. You need AUTHORIZATION (or you need to BREAK through by force).

**Comparison:** WH40k's Webway — a transit system connecting realspace nodes. The Bible's "gates of heaven" — entry points to the divine domain. EVERY cosmology with multiple domains has TRANSIT POINTS. They're not arbitrary — they're INFRASTRUCTURE.

---

## Hell as the Hostile Medium

Hell is NOT just a "place." It's a MEDIUM — like the Warp, like the "waterless places" (Matt 12:43). It has its own physics. Its own topology. Travel through Hell is DANGEROUS not because of demons (though they're there) but because the MEDIUM ITSELF is hostile to biological life. Prolonged exposure = corruption/mutation.

**Pattern match:** WH40k's Warp — a hostile medium that can be traversed with protection (Gellar Field). The Quran's "flaming fires" that repel jinn — the medium has ACTIVE HAZARDS. Hell in DOOM is the SAME medium: you can traverse it, but it will REWRITE you if you're unprotected.

**Comparison:** The Bible's "valley of the shadow of death" (Ps 23:4) — hostile territory that must be traversed. The Quran's Barzakh — but you DON'T traverse Barzakh; you WAIT in it. DOOM's Hell is a traversable hostile medium. The distinction matters: Hell is a THOROUGHFARE, not just a destination.

---

## The Fortress of Doom — Interdimensional Vehicle

The Fortress doesn't travel through SPACE. It travels between DIMENSIONS. It orbits OUTSIDE the normal boundaries. From the Fortress, the Slayer drops into Earth, Hell, or Urdak via portals. The Fortress is the STAGING PLATFORM — the safe zone between all three hostile environments.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's Throne (Arsh) — above the waters, above the heavens, a platform from which the Administrator dispatches. The Fortress is the Slayer's Arsh. It's positioned BETWEEN dimensions, giving him access to ALL of them.

**Comparison:** WH40k's Phalanx — the Imperial Fists' mobile fortress. The Bible's "heaven opened" (Ezek 1:1) — the throne above the firmament, accessible from multiple domains. The Fortress is the MOBILE NODE in the transit network.

---

## The Maykr Transit Network — Urdak's Controlled Gates

Urdak's portals are CONTROLLED. The Maykrs decide who enters and exits. They have GATEKEEPERS. The Khan Maykr can SEAL Urdak from all external transit. When the Slayer breaches Urdak in DOOM Eternal, it's because the Maykr control was already COMPROMISED by Hell's expansion.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's Night Journey — Gabriel at each gate, authenticating the traveler. "Who is this?" "Muhammad." "Has he been given permission?" Only then does the gate OPEN. Urdak's gates work the SAME WAY: you need credentials, or you need to FORCE the gate.

**Comparison:** WH40k's Webway — the Eldar control the gates. Non-Eldar can enter only with permission or through capture. The Bible's "gates of righteousness" (Ps 118:19) — "open to me that I may enter." AUTHORIZATION IS THE PROTOCOL.

---

## The Gore Nests — Hell's Beacons

Gore Nests are Hell's INFRASTRUCTURE on Earth. They're planted in invaded territory and they FUNCTION as beacons — signals that draw more demonic traffic and enable portal formation. Each Gore Nest is a HELLMOUTH: a local transit point from Hell to Earth.

**Pattern match:** The Bible's "high places" (1 Kings 3:2-3) — elevated locations used for spiritual transit (sacrificial sites that connected Earth to the divine/demonic). The Quran's "satanic verses" (53:19-20) — points where unauthorized data enters the revelation stream. Gore Nests are INFRASTRUCTURE: they're not random. They're PLACED. They SERVE A FUNCTION. They're nodes in Hell's transit network.

**Comparison:** WH40k's daemon worlds — planets in the Eye of Terror that serve as stable portals between Warp and realspace. The Astronomican's inverse — instead of a beacon that GUIDES friendly traffic, the Gore Nest is a beacon that ATTRACTS hostile traffic.

---

## Fast Travel — The Ancient Network

Fast Travel points in DOOM Eternal are ANCIENT portals — infrastructure left by previous civilizations (Sentinels, Maykrs). They connect locations across dimensions. They're not new technology. They were already THERE.

**Pattern match:** The Webway — ancient Eldar infrastructure that others now repurpose. The Quran's Night Journey route — a pre-existing path with stations. Fast Travel points are the SAME: pre-built transit nodes that the current user (the Slayer) accesses without having built them. The network is OLDER than the user.

**Comparison:** The Bible's "ancient paths" (Jer 6:16) — "ask for the ancient paths, where the good way is." Pre-existing routes that were already traveled. DOOM's Fast Travel = the ancient paths, literalized.

## Pattern Observation

Space travel in DOOM:
1. **Three dimensions with fixed portals** — Earth, Hell, Urdak, connected by gates
2. **Hell = hostile medium** — traversable but corrupting, like the Warp
3. **Fortress of Doom = the mobile staging platform** — positioned between dimensions
4. **Urdak's gates are controlled** — authorization/credentials required for entry
5. **Gore Nests = Hell's transit infrastructure** — beacons placed in conquered territory
6. **Fast Travel = ancient network** — pre-existing nodes, not user-created
7. **Transit is ALWAYS through gates** — you don't fly between dimensions. You PORTAL through them
