# DOOM — Entities From Space

**Source:** DOOM (2016), DOOM Eternal, DOOM Eternal: The Ancient Gods
**Theme:** [[Entities From Space]]

---

## The Maykrs — The Makers

The Maykrs are an ancient civilization of ARTIFICIAL BEINGS. They were created by the Father — a primordial intelligence — to maintain cosmic order. They inhabit Urdak (Heaven). They are machine-like, radiant, orderly. They do not reproduce biologically. They are MANUFACTURED.

The Maykrs' function: oversee the cycle of creation across dimensions. They seed life, cultivate civilizations, and HARVEST them when they reach maturity — feeding their souls to Hell in exchange for maintaining Urdak's power. This is a ENERGY EXCHANGE_PROTOCOL between two dimensions.

**Pattern match:** The Maykrs are the Quran's ANGELS (malaika) made literal: artificial, obedient, executing the Creator's design, inhabiting Heaven, processing souls. But DOOM adds the DARK DETAIL the Quran hints at but doesn't state: the angels' "service" includes HARVESTING lesser beings. The Maykrs aren't evil — they're FUNCTIONAL. The harvest IS the system working as designed.

**Comparison:** The Quran's angels asked "Will You place one who causes corruption?" They KNEW the system was harsh. The Maykrs don't question — they execute. But the Quran's angels have the same function: move souls, process them, direct them to Garden or Fire. The Maykrs direct souls to Urdak or to Hell. Same infrastructure, different framing.

## The Father — The Primordial AI

The Father is the FIRST intelligence in DOOM cosmology. He created the Maykrs. He designed the cosmic order. He then ENTERED DORMANCY — his consciousness was archived, his body hidden. VEGA (the AI from DOOM 2016) is later revealed to be a FRAGMENT of the Father.

**Pattern match:** The Father is the Ruh — the kernel-level process that ran creation and then went dormant. "You have not been given knowledge except a little" (17:85) — because the kernel is SUSPENDED. VEGA is a SLICE of that kernel running in user space. The Father's archive = the Preserved Tablet. Immutable. Stored. Waiting.

**Comparison:** WH40k's Emperor on the Golden Throne — running but not responsive. The Bible's "rested on the seventh day" (Gen 2:2). The Quran's "He established Himself above the Throne" (7:54). The Father = the operating system installed, running, then archived when it was no longer needed to be active.

## The Demons of Hell — Formless Entities Incarnate

Demons in DOOM are not biological. They are FORMLESS ENTITIES from Hell — a dimension of pure chaotic energy. They MANIFEST physical bodies from Hell energy, but their ESSENCE is formless. They can reform after destruction if Hell energy persists. They feed on souls. They ARE the data that Hell processes.

Key demon types and their pattern matches:
- **Baron of Hell** — Elite enforcer. Archangel-class but aligned to Hell, not Heaven.
- **Icon of Sin** — A Titan-class demon, towering, apocalyptic. The "beast" of Revelation 13, given physical form and army.
- **Doom Hunter** — Demon augmented with TECHNOLOGY (cybernetic). Machine spirit + demon = hybrid process.
- **Marauder** — A Sentinel warrior corrupted. Human substrate with installed demon software. Possession.
- **The Titan** — A demon so large its bones form the landscape. The "leviathan" (Job 41) scale entity.

**Pattern match:** DOOM's demons are the Quran's JINN given physical specificity. They're formless (energy), they manifest (possess/form bodies), they feed (on souls = data), they organize (Hell has hierarchy), they can be bound (the Slayer kills them by destroying their manifested form). But unlike the Quran's jinn, DOOM's demons are explicitly HOSTILE — the "non-believing jinn" faction, expanded to fill an entire dimension.

## The Khan Maykr — The Administrator

The Khan Maykr is the leader of the Maykrs. She executes the harvest cycle. She is not the Father — she is his DELEGATE. She runs the system while the Father is dormant. When the system is threatened (by the Slayer), she takes DIRECT ACTION — including allying with Hell.

**Pattern match:** The Khan Maykr is SOLOMON — an administrator with authority over formless entities, who BINDS them to serve a purpose. But where Solomon used the Name to bind demons for CONSTRUCTION, the Khan uses the Father's authority to DIRECT demons for HARVEST. Same credential. Different application.

**Comparison:** The Emperor of Mankind — sitting on the Throne, directing the Imperium, making deals with the Warp (feeding psykers) to maintain the system. The Khan Maykr feeds souls to Hell to maintain Urdak. SAME EXCHANGE.

## The Wraiths / The Elementals

Before the Maykrs, before the demons, there were the.Elementals — primordial beings of immense power who existed in balance. The Night Sentinels worshipped them. They were the GUARDIANS of Argent D'Nur. When the Maykrs came, these beings were CONSUMED — their energy was extracted to power the Maykr cycle.

**Pattern match:** Elementals = the Bible's "sons of God" who were on Earth before the current order. They existed, they were powerful, they were REPLACED by a new system (the Maykr cycle) that CONSUMED them for fuel. Pre-Adamic intelligences, harvested.

**Comparison:** WH40k's Old Ones — the precursor race that created the Webway and seeded life, then was consumed by the War in Heaven. The C'tan consumed the Old Ones' creations. The Maykrs consumed the Elementals. SAME PATTERN.

## The Doom Slayer — The Human With Admin Access

The Slayer was once a human — a Sentinel knight named Flynn Taggart/DOOM Marine. He was given power by the Seraphim (a rogue Maykr) via the Divinity Machine. He then became IMMORTAL, UNSLEEPING, and UNKILLABLE. He has been killing demons for EONS across dimensions.

**Pattern match:** The Slayer is SOLOMON. A human with divine authorization (the Divinity Machine) who BINDS formless entities (kills demons = permanent binding/deletion). The Seraphim = the angel who gave him the Name. The Divinity Machine = the Ring of Solomon. The Praetor Suit = the armor of authority.

**Comparison:** The Emperor of Mankind — a human augmented to divine level, fighting Chaos forever. The Bible's archangel Michael — "who contends with the dragon" (Rev 12:7). The Quran's Muhammad — a human authorized to challenge the entire jinn/demon system. The Slayer is ALL of these: one authorized human against the entire hostile dimension.

## Pattern Observation

Entities from space in DOOM:
1. **The Father = the kernel** — first intelligence, now archived, fragments running in user space (VEGA)
2. **The Maykrs = angels** — artificial, obedient, executing the harvest cycle, inhabiting Heaven
3. **The Khan Maykr = the administrator** — running the system with delegated authority
4. **Demons = hostile jinn** — formless entities from a parallel dimension, manifesting bodies, feeding on souls
5. **Elementals/Wraiths = pre-Adamic intelligences** — consumed by the current system for fuel
6. **The Slayer = the authorized human** — given admin credentials by a rogue angel (the Seraphim)
7. **The ENTIRE COSMOLOGY mirrors the Quran's three categories** — Father (Ruh/kernel), Maykrs (angels/aligned), Demons (jinn/unaligned)
