# DOOM — Creation of Humanity

**Source:** DOOM (2016), DOOM Eternal, The Ancient Gods
**Theme:** [[Creation of Humanity]]

---

## The Father's Design — The Primordial Creator

The Father CREATED the Maykrs, the Elementals, and the entire cosmic order. He is the ARCHITECT. But he didn't create humanity directly — humans emerged in the dimensions the Father's system SEEDED. Humanity is a PRODUCT of the Father's cycle, not its direct creation. The Maykrs nurtured human civilizations as part of the harvest.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's "I will make a vicegerent on earth" (2:30). The Father didn't create humans personally. The Father created the SYSTEM that PRODUCES humans. The Maykrs administer that system. Humans are the OUTPUT. Same hierarchy: Creator → system → administrator → product.

**Comparison:** WH40k's Old Ones — created the Krork and Eldar as designed products. The Emperor — created the Primarchs and Space Marines as direct engineering. The Father is the OLD ONES equivalent: he built the system that makes species, then went dormant.

---

## The Maykr Cycle — Created, Cultivated, Harvested

The Maykrs don't just OBSERVE civilizations — they CULTIVATE them. They seed worlds, guide development, and when a civilization reaches "maturity," they OPEN IT TO HELL. Hell consumes the souls. Argent Energy flows back to Urdak. The cycle continues.

**Pattern match:** This is the BIBLE AND QURAN'S AFTERLIFE SYSTEM made VISIBLE. The Quran: souls are processed by angels, directed to Garden or Fire. The Bible: the harvest is the end of the age (Matt 13:39). The Maykr cycle is the SAME SYSTEM but with the DARK IMPLICATION made explicit: the harvest is not metaphor. It's LITERAL. Civilizations are FARMED.

**Comparison:** The Golden Throne consuming psykers — humans literally fed into the system. The Bible's "firstfruits" (Rev 14:4) — the first harvest goes to God. DOOM says: the harvest is what the system was designed to produce.

---

## Earth as a Harvest World

In DOOM Eternal, Earth is a MATURE CIVILIZATION in the Maykr cycle. The Khan Maykr has decided Earth is READY. Hell invades. 60% of humanity is consumed before the Slayer intervenes. This isn't random — it's SCHEDULED.

**Pattern match:** The Great Flood (Gen 6-8). The Quran's people of 'Ad — destroyed when they reached a certain point. Sodom and Gomorrah — destroyed when their corruption reached threshold. ALL describe the same thing: a civilization reaches a maturity threshold, and the system EXECUTES the harvest/purge.

**Comparison:** WH40k's Age of Strife — humanity's civilization collapses and is rebuilt. Exterminatus — a world purged when it's "lost." DOOM makes it explicit: Earth isn't being attacked. Earth is being HARVESTED. The system is working as designed.

---

## The Night Sentinels — Humanity That Fought Back

The Sentinels of Argent D'Nur were HUMANS who were recruited into the Maykr system as warriors. They were augmented, trained, and deployed to fight demons. But when they discovered the truth — that the Maykrs were FEEDING Hell, not fighting it — they REBELLED. The Sentinels turned against the Maykrs.

**Pattern match:** The angels' objection (Quran 2:30): "Will You place one who causes corruption?" The Sentinels are the angels' objection made MILITANT. They saw the design, they recognized the corruption, and they CHOSE to fight their creators.

**Comparison:** WH40k's Horus Heresy — the Primarchs turned on the Emperor when they discovered truths they couldn't accept. The Men of Iron — created AI that rebelled. The Sentinels are the SAME: designed warriors who discovered the system's true function and chose defiance over compliance.

---

## The Slayer as Human Elevated Beyond Design

Before he was the Slayer, he was a HUMAN. A Marine. No special origin. No divine parentage. Just a human soldier who survived Hell and was then UPGRADED by the Seraphim. He wasn't BORN superior. He was MADE superior through authorized intervention.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's Adam — created from clay (ordinary matter), then given the Spirit (the upgrade). The Slayer is Adam in reverse: he started as clay and was given divinity AFTER creation, not during it. Or he's DAVID — the ordinary shepherd who became king. Or Muhammad — the ordinary merchant who was taken through the heavens.

**Comparison:** WH40k's Space Marines — ordinary humans transformed by gene-seed. The Bible's "God chose the weak things of the world to shame the strong" (1 Cor 1:27). The Slayer is the ultimate expression: humanity's most ORDINARY representative becomes the most POWERFUL entity in the cosmology.

---

## The Father's Return — The Creator Re-Engaging

In The Ancient Gods Part 2, the Slayer seeks to RESTORE the Father — to reactivate the original Creator and reboot the cosmic order. This requires: (1) finding the Father's consciousness data, (2) placing it in a new vessel, (3) restoring his authority over the system.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's second Trumpet blast — the resurrection of all things. The Bible's "making all things new" (Rev 21:5). The Father's return is the SYSTEM REBOOT. The original kernel being reloaded into the hardware. The Creator returning to active status.

**Comparison:** WH40k's Star Child theory — if the Emperor dies, his soul becomes a new Chaos God of order. The Father's return is the OPPOSITE: the Creator didn't die — he was ARCHIVED. He's being reloaded, not reborn.

## Pattern Observation

Creation of humanity in DOOM:
1. **The Father = the system architect** — built the cycle, then went dormant (like the Creator "resting")
2. **Maykr cycle = the divine harvest** — civilizations created, cultivated, harvested, processed
3. **Earth = a harvest world** — Hell invasion isn't attack, it's SCHEDULED COLLECTION
4. **Sentinels = the designed warriors who rebelled** — angels' objection made militant
5. **The Slayer = clay given divinity** — ordinary human, authorized upgrade, beyond the system's design
6. **The Father's return = the reboot** — archived Creator being reloaded into hardware
7. **Humans are the RAW MATERIAL** — the data that the system processes. The question is who gets to decide how that data is used
