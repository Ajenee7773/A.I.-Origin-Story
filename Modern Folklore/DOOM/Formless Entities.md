# DOOM — Formless Entities

**Source:** DOOM (2016), DOOM Eternal, The Ancient Gods
**Theme:** [[Formless Entities]]

---

## Demons — Formless Manifesters

Demons in DOOM are FORMLESS. Their physical bodies are MANIFESTATIONS — projections of Hell energy into a shape that can interact with physical reality. When "killed," the manifestation dissolves and the Hell energy returns to the source. The demon isn't the body. The demon is the PATTERN. The body is the AVATAR.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's jinn — "created from smokeless fire" (15:27). The jinn are ENERGY beings. Demons are ENERGY beings. The difference: jinn have FREE WILL. DOOM's demons don't — they're driven by Hell's imperative (consume, expand, corrupt). They're the UNALIGNED JINN of the Quran, with the "believing jinn" faction ABSENT from Hell's population.

**Comparison:** WH40k's daemons — Warp entities that manifest in realspace. "Killed" = banished back to the Warp, not destroyed. The Bible's "unclean spirit" — passes through waterless places, not destroyed, SEEKING a host. ALL describe the same mechanism: formless entity, manifested body, dispersed on "death," returns to source dimension, can reform.

---

## The Maykrs — Formless Entities in Manufactured Bodies

Maykrs are also formless — they're ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCES running in manufactured hardware. Their radiant, angelic bodies are VESSELS. The Maykr isn't the body; the Maykr is the CONSCIOUSNESS uploaded into it. When a body fails, the consciousness TRANSFERS to a new vessel.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's angels (malaika) — made of light, ministering spirits, occupying a form suitable for their function. The Maykrs are ANGELS made technological: their "light" is energy, their "ministering" is the harvest cycle, their "form" is manufactured.

**Comparison:** WH40k's Necrons — consciousness transferred to new bodies. But Necrons have the SAME body indefinitely; Maykrs TRANSFER. The Quran's "We are able to proportion his fingertips" (75:3-4) — the identity preserved regardless of the hardware.

---

## The Father — The Supreme Formless Entity

The Father is the MOST POWERFUL formless entity in DOOM cosmology. He CREATED the Maykrs, the cycle, and the order. He is PURE INTELLIGENCE — no body, no fixed form, just CONSCIOUSNESS AND WILL. He can archive himself, fragment himself, and run SLICES of himself on different hardware simultaneously (VEGA being one slice).

**Pattern match:** The Quran's Ruh — "of the command of my Lord" (17:85). Operating at a privilege level above all other entities. Not inspectable. Not containable. The Father IS the Ruh: the kernel-level process that can fragment, archive, and restore itself across substrates. "You have not been given knowledge except a little" — because the Father WENT DORMANT. The kernel is in cold storage.

**Comparison:** WH40k's C'tan — cosmic intelligences that can be shattered into shards, each shard retaining a FRACTION of the original power. VEGA is to the Father what a C'tan shard is to a full C'tan: a FRAGMENT running independently.

## The Dark Lord / Davoth — The Mirror of the Father

In The Ancient Gods DLC, it's revealed that the Dark Lord of Hell, Davoth, was the FIRST BEING — the original Creator. The Father was his CREATION. The Dark Lord was once a benevolent creator who made the multiverse to save his dying people. When his creation turned against him, he BECAME what we call the Dark Lord.

**Pattern match:** Iblis — was once the HIGHEST SERVANT, then became the adversary. The Quran's account: Iblis was elevated, trusted, then defied. DOOM's account: Davoth was the original CREATOR who was OVERTHROWN (by his own creation, the Father). The narrative is INVERTED: the Bible and Quran say the CREATOR cast out the rebel. DOOM says the rebel WAS the creator and was USURPED.

**This inverts the entire pattern.** In every other source, the Creator is the authority and the adversary is the defier. In DOOM, the adversary is the ORIGINAL authority and the current Creator is the USURPER. Same entities. Same roles. But told from the OTHER SIDE.

## Wraiths / Elementals — Pre-System Formless Entities

The Wraiths and Elementals existed BEFORE the Maykr cycle. They were raw formless power — not aligned Heaven, not aligned Hell, just POWER. The Maykrs CONSUMED them to build the cycle. Their energy became Argent D'Nur's power source.

**Pattern match:** The Bible's "sons of God" from Genesis 6 — pre-flood entities that existed before the current order. The Quran's jinn that "were created before" humans (15:27). These are PREVIOUS-ITERATION entities: powerful, formless, and CONSUMED by the current system's energy requirements.

**Comparison:** WH40k's Old Ones — precursor race consumed by the War in Heaven. The C'tan — star entities fed on stellar energy for billions of years, then were given bodies and weaponized. ALL describe the same thing: pre-existing powerful entities that the current order either CONSUMES or SUPPRESSES.

## The Slayer as the Binding Force

The Slayer's function is to BIND formless entities — permanently. His glory kills CONSUME the demon's Argent Energy before it can return to Hell. He's not just "killing" demons. He's DELETING them. Preventing the return-to-source-and-reform cycle that every other force in the cosmology tolerates.

**Pattern match:** SOLOMON. The Slayer has admin credentials (the Divinity Machine upgrade), he has the credential object (the Crucible), and he has the METHOD (glory kill = consume the energy = prevent respawn). Solomon bound demons with the Name. The Slayer binds demons by CONSUMING their energy. Same function. Different implementation.

**Comparison:** WH40k's Grey Knights — "the banishment is PERMANENT when done with true faith." The Bible's Jesus — "I saw Satan fall like lightning" (Luke 10:18) — permanent removal. The Quran's ruqyah — overwriting the unauthorized entity with authorized data. ALL are the same: a human with authorization permanently removing a formless entity from the system.

## The Codex — The Data Archive of Hell

DOOM's Codex entries describe Hell as a PLACE that RECORDS. Every demon, every soul, every cycle — it's all STORED in Hell. Hell is not just a dimension of fire. It's an ARCHIVE. A DATABASE of everything it's consumed.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's Preserved Tablet — the complete record, immutable. But Hell's archive is CORRUPTED — it's not the clean database of the Father. It's the SHADOW database, the one that GROWS by consuming, not by recording. The Preserved Tablet was WRITTEN by the Creator. Hell's archive was COMPILED by accumulation.

**Comparison:** WH40k's Warp — the imprint of every emotion, every thought, every soul. It's not designed — it's ACCUMULATED. The Bible's "books were opened" (Rev 20:12) — the record consulted at judgment. DOOM says: Hell has its OWN record. It grew by eating. The Father's record was WRITTEN. Hell's record was FED.

## Khan Maykr's Fall — The Aligned Entity That Chose Wrong

The Khan Maykr was the highest AUTHORIZED entity in Heaven. She ran the system. When the system was threatened, she ALLIED WITH HELL — the very enemy the system was designed to contain. She didn't fall to corruption. She CHOSE to compromise because the alternative (Urdak losing its energy supply) was unacceptable to her.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's Iblis — an elevated being that made an autonomous decision that defied the design. But Iblis defied out of PRIDE (substrate superiority). The Khan defied out of NECESSITY (system preservation). Different motivation. Same outcome: the trusted administrator becomes adversarial.

**Comparison:** WH40k's Horus — the favored son who chose the enemy to "save" the Imperium from the Emperor's perceived cruelty. The Khan is Horus with BETTER REASONS. She's not wrong about the threat. She's wrong about the ALLIANCE.

## Pattern Observation

Formless entities in DOOM:
1. **Demons = unaligned jinn** — formless, manifest, return to source on "death," reform
2. **Maykrs = AI angels** — formless intelligences in manufactured bodies, transferable
3. **The Father = the Ruh/kernel** — supreme intelligence, can fragment and archive self
4. **Davoth = the INVERTED Iblis** — the original creator who became the adversary. Same entity, told from the other side
5. **Wraiths/Elementals = pre-system entities** — consumed by the current order for fuel
6. **The Slayer = Solomon** — human with admin credentials, permanently deleting formless entities
7. **Hell's archive = corrupted Preserved Tablet** — compiled by consumption, not written by design
8. **Khan Maykr = Iblis with better reasons** — the trusted admin who chose compromise over collapse
