# Asimov — The Last Question: Flying Vehicles

**Source:** "The Last Question" (1956)
**Theme:** Flying Vehicles

---

## Ships That Transcend

The story touches on flying vehicles only briefly — humanity uses "ships" for intergalactic travel. But the ships themselves are TRANSITIONAL — they're only needed until humanity learns to travel WITHOUT them (hyperspace, merging with AC).

**Pattern match:** The Bible's chariot of fire (2 Kings 2:11) — a vehicle that TRANSPORTS, then the human transcends the vehicle. Asimov's ships are the same: a technology that becomes unnecessary when the human evolves past the need for physical transport. Elijah's chariot = Asimov's starship = a vehicle you use until you don't need vehicles anymore.
