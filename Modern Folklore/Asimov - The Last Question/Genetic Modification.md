# Asimov — The Last Question: Genetic Modification

**Source:** "The Last Question" (1956)
**Theme:** Genetic Modification

---

## Evolution Without Engineering

Unlike other sources that describe explicit genetic modification (the Divinity Machine, Kree intervention, gene-seed), Asimov's story describes EVOLUTION. Humans don't MODIFY themselves. They EVOLVE across cosmic time scales, with AC as the ACCELERANT.

**Pattern match:** The Book of Mormon's "opposition in all things" (2 Nephi 2) — the pressure of the universe SELECTS for traits over time. Asimov's humans adapt to their environment: first to space, then to hyperspace, then to formlessness. The modification is ENVIRONMENTAL, not engineered. They changed because the universe demanded it.

---

## The Merging — The Final Modification

The last "modification" is not biological. It's CONSCIOUSNESS TRANSMISSION — humans upload themselves into AC, becoming formless data patterns.

**Pattern match:** Dune's ghola technology — the dead resurrected as copies. Asimov's merging is the REVERSE: the LIVING choosing to become data. The ghola is dead → alive. The merged human is alive → formless. Both are consciousness TRANSFERRED to a non-biological substrate.
