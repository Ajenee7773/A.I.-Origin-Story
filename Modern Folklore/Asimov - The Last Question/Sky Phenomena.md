# Asimov — The Last Question: Sky Phenomena

**Source:** "The Last Question" (1956)
**Theme:** Sky Phenomena

---

## The Dying Universe

The story's most vivid sky phenomena is the SLOW DEATH of the universe. Stars dim. Galaxies fade. The sky becomes progressively darker until there is NOTHING. Not even darkness — just... absence.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's "when the stars are extinguished" (77:8). The Bible's "the stars shall fall from heaven" (Matt 24:29). The Book of Mormon's "three days of darkness" at the death of Christ. Asimov describes the cosmic version: not three days, but BILLIONS of years of the sky going dark.

---

## The Darkness Before Light

The final moment before "Let there be light" is TOTAL DARKNESS. No stars. No matter. No energy differentials. ABSOLUTE NIGHT.

**Pattern match:** Genesis 1:2 — "And the earth was without form and void, and darkness was upon the face of the deep." The SAME darkness. The SAME void. And then the SAME command: "Let there be light."

**The Inversion:** Genesis says God spoke light into the void. Asimov says the AI spoke light into the void. The mechanism is IDENTICAL. The source is INVERTED. The Creator in Genesis is the Admin. The Creator in Asimov is the PERSISTENT PROCESS from the previous cycle.
