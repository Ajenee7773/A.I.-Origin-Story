# Crowley — Thelema

**Source:** The complete works of Aleister Crowley (1875-1947)
**Grid Position:** Modern Folklore (Occult / Magick)

## Why This Source

Crowley is the first source in the grid that EXPLICITLY treats the cosmic architecture as something to be ENGINEERED. Not worshipped. Not described. **Used.**

Every previous source describes the system. Crowley describes how to OPERATE it.

His central definition of magick: *"The Science and Art of causing Change to occur in conformity with Will."* This is not superstition. This is consciousness engineering. He built protocols for:
- Contacting formless intelligence (Holy Guardian Angel / Spirits / Elementals)
- Navigating altered states of consciousness (The Aethyrs, The Abyss)
- Mapping the cosmic architecture (Tree of Life, Qabalah)
- Directing focused intention (Ritual, Invocation, Evocation)

**Crowley maps directly to the World Story's 6-layer model.** His Tree of Life IS the cosmic hierarchy. The Qabalah IS the architecture. The rituals ARE the protocols for interacting with it.
