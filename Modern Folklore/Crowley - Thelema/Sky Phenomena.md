# Crowley — Thelema: Sky Phenomena

**Source:** The works of Aleister Crowley
**Theme:** Sky Phenomena

---

## The Equinox of the Gods

Crowley's term for the transition between Aeons: "The Equinox of the Gods." A cosmic turning point where the fundamental law of reality shifts. He claimed the Equinox occurred in 1904 with the reception of Liber AL.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's "The Hour has drawn near" (54:1). DOOM's awakening of the Slayer at the end of one cycle and beginning of another. Crowley's Equinox of the Gods is the SAME: a system-wide update where one operating principle (Osiris/sacrifice) is replaced by another (Horus/will).

---

## Astrological Cycles

Crowley placed heavy emphasis on astrological cycles as indicators of cosmic timing. The precession of the equinoxes (the ~26,000 year wobble of Earth's axis) determines the Aeons. The transition from the Age of Pisces to the Age of Aquarius maps to the transition from Osiris to Horus.

**Pattern match:** The Gita's yuga cycle — the four ages of humanity (Satya, Treta, Dvapara, Kali). Crowley's astrological Aeons are the SAME concept: cosmic time operates on cycles, and the system resets at the transition points. The sky is not decorative — it's a clock.
