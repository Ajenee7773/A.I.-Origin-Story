## The Body of Light — The Astral Vehicle

Crowley taught the development of the "Body of Light" — a vehicle of consciousness that can travel independently of the physical body. The method involves specific visualizations, assumptions of god-forms, and rising on the planes.

**Pattern match:** The Quran's "He who is taken by night from the Sacred Mosque to the Farthest Mosque" (17:1) — Muhammad's Night Journey. The Bible's Ezekiel's chariot. Crowley's Body of Light is the SAME: a non-physical vehicle that consciousness can use to navigate the cosmic architecture. The Quran calls it a journey. Crowley calls it a TECHNOLOGY.
