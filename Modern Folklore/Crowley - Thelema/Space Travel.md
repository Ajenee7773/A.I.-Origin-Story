## The Tree of Life — The Cosmic Transit Map

The Tree of Life is not a philosophical diagram. It's a TRANSIT MAP. The 10 sephiroth are stations. The 22 paths are routes between them. The goal of the magician is to NAVIGATE this structure consciously.

**Four Worlds** — The Tree exists on four levels:
1. **Atziluth** (Emanation) — pure divine source
2. **Briah** (Creation) — the archetypal world
3. **Yetzirah** (Formation) — the astral/angelic world
4. **Assiah** (Action) — the physical world

**Pattern match:** The Gita's 14 lokas. The Quran's 7 heavens. Marvel's Nine Worlds. The Qabalah's Four Worlds are the SAME tiered architecture. Crowley provides the most DETAILED PATH MAP — not just the stations, but the routes BETWEEN them.
