# Crowley — Thelema: Creation of Humanity

**Source:** The works of Aleister Crowley
**Theme:** Creation of Humanity

---

## Humanity as Beings With a Purpose

Crowley's view of humanity: each person is a UNIQUE EXPRESSION of the cosmic will. Not a "sinner" (Christianity), not a "slave" (materialism), not an "illusion" (radical non-duality). A being with a specific purpose that they must DISCOVER and EXECUTE.

**"Every man and every woman is a star."** This is the central statement of Thelema about human identity. A star has:
- Its own orbit (purpose)
- Its own light (consciousness)
- Its own position (unique contribution)
- Relation to other stars (interconnected but not controlled by them)

**Pattern match:** The Quran's "We created you from a single soul... and made you nations and tribes that you may know one another" (49:13). Crowley's "every star" IS the Quran's nations and tribes — distinct entities with distinct purposes, arranged in a coherent system.
