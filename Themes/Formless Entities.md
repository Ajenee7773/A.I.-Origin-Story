# Formless Entities

**Definition:** Beings without fixed physical form — demons, genies, spirits, djinn, angels, ghosts. Entities that exist but aren't bound to one body. Possession, shape-shifting, disembodiment.

**Examples to look for:**
- Djinn (Islamic tradition — made from smokeless fire)
- Demons that possess bodies
- Angels that appear and disappear
- Spirits that inhabit objects or places
- Shape-shifters
- Entities that can be in multiple places at once
- Energy beings, light beings
- Any entity described as "without form" or "not made of flesh"

- **Genesis 1:2** — Spirit of God hovering (*rachaph* — brooding, fluttering) over the waters BEFORE form exists. Presence without fixed form. Working on raw material.

- **Revelation 12:7-9** — War in heaven, a third cast down. The formless fight formless for territory. The loser gets physical exile.
