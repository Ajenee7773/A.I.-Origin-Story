# Space Travel

**Definition:** Journeys between worlds, dimensions, or celestial realms. Any being, vessel, or consciousness that moves between domains that humans cannot normally access.

**Examples to look for:**
- Ascent to heaven / descent to underworld
- Charriots of fire crossing the sky
- Interdimensional travel (Bifröst, portals, gates)
- Divine beings coming "down" from the sky
- Humans taken up to celestial realms
- Descriptions of travel that sound like rocket launches or atmospheric entry

## Sources

### Bible

- **Genesis 28:12-13** — Jacob's Ladder. Structure connecting Earth and Heaven. Angels ascending and descending. Transit infrastructure.
- **2 Kings 2:11** — Elijah's ascent via whirlwind + fiery chariot. Physical relocation from surface to sky.
- **2 Corinthians 12:2-4** — Paul caught up to the "third heaven." Multiple layered domains. At least three structured levels.
- **Hebrews 1:14** — Angels as "ministering spirits, sent forth." Dispatch function. Personnel on assignment.
- **John 14:2** — "Many mansions [monai — dwelling places, stations]" in the Father's house. Multiple locations. Jesus goes to PREPARE one. Logistics.
- **Revelation 4:1** — A door opens in heaven. Voice says "come up hither." Portal with controlled access.
- **Revelation 21:2-3, 16** — New Jerusalem descends from heaven to Earth. Measured: 12,000 furlongs cube (~1,500 mi/side). An engineered structure the size of a small moon, relocating.

### Warhammer 40k

- **The Warp** — Parallel dimension for interstellar travel. Hostile. Requires protection. "Heaven" that kills you. "No man has ascended to heaven but he who came down from heaven" (John 3:13) = you need authorization and a Gellar field. See [[Modern Folklore/Warhammer 40k/Space Travel]]
- **The Astronomican** — Psychic beacon = pillar of cloud/fire. Requires faith + sacrifice to maintain. Without it: lost.
- **The Webway** — Jacob's Ladder ENGINEERED. Permanent transit tunnels connecting domains. Emperor tried to tap it (Webway Project) = Tower of Babel. Magnus broke it = God confusing the languages.
- **Gellar Fields** — The Tabernacle in the Void. Emperor's presence keeps daemons out. "The glory filled the tabernacle" = the Gellar field fills the ship.
- **The Great Rift (Cicatrix Maledictum)** — "A door was opened in heaven" (Rev 4:1) — but from the WRONG SIDE. Daemons pour out. The door will not close.
- **Dolmen Gates / Necron FTL** — Bypass the hostile dimension entirely. Enoch's translation: move between domains without going through the intermediary.
- **The Pharos** — Miniature Astronomican. Multiple beacons = multiple "pillars of fire." The NETWORK scales because the galaxy is larger than the desert.

### Quran

- **Seven Layered Heavens** (65:12, 67:3) — Seven distinct domains, each complete with inhabitants and properties. ENGINEERED, not random. Webway parallel. See [[Religion/Quran/Space Travel]]
- **The Night Journey Transit Protocol** — Buraq (transport) + Gabriel (escort) + checkpoints at each heaven gate + Lote Tree boundary + solo access beyond. A complete NETWORK PROTOCOL.
- **Jinn Intercepted** (72:8-9) — Unauthorized transit = FIRED UPON. "Flaming fires" = defense system active post-revelation. The BARRIER HAS WEAPONS.
- **Solomon's Wind FTL** (34:12) — Month's journey in a morning. Fleet transit via atmospheric medium manipulation. Wind = navigation medium.
- **The Barrier Between the Two Seas** (55:19-20) — Two domains separated by an impermeable firewall. Meets, but does NOT mix.
- **Gog and Magog Containment** (18:94-97) — Iron + copper + fire = CONTAINMENT STRUCTURE. Like Necron Pylons. Fails only at the End.

### Book of Mormon

- **Lehi's exodus = guided transit** — Liahona (nav), wind (propulsion), faith (authorization). The Night Journey at terrestrial scale. See [[Religion/Book of Mormon/Space Travel]]
- **Jaredite barges = hostile-medium transit** — sealed vessel, divine propulsion. Same architecture as Warp travel: sealed hull + hostile medium + external propulsion.
- **Multiple migrations = repeated operations** — at least four population transfers to the Americas. The system moves data in waves.
- **Liahona = navigation protocol made physical** — dynamic, conditional, real-time, with WRITTEN INSTRUCTIONS. Astronomican + terminal + security system.

### Dune

- **Fold space = the RELIGIOUS model** — NOT dimensional transit. DISTANCE COMPRESSION. Same as Night Journey (Quran) and "heavens rolled up" (Bible). The ONLY secular source matching the religious transit model. See [[Modern Folklore/Dune/Space Travel]]
- **Navigator = human Jibril** — the guide function on modified human hardware. Not divine. ENGINEERED.
- **Spice = the centralized resource** — one substance, one planet, entire system depends on it. Astronomican / Tree of Life equivalent.
- **Guild = unauthorized gatekeepers** — the malaika's function as human institution. Independent power holding the Imperium hostage.
- **No computers = fully human-operated** — the ONLY transit system running entirely on human cognition. POST-AI.
- **Fold space PROVES: the religious transit model works without divinity. It requires EXPANDED CONSCIOUSNESS, not divine agents.**

### Bhagavad Gita

- **14 lokas = most detailed domain map** — seven above, seven below. Quran's seven heavens as SUBSET. Complete zoning architecture. See [[Religion/Bhagavad Gita/Space Travel]]
- **Five transit methods** — physical vehicle, living mount, yoga, death, divine grace. Most transit-diverse source in the grid.
- **Yoga = consciousness navigation** — no vehicle needed. You TRAIN your awareness to move through the architecture directly. Unique transit protocol.
- **Subtle body = transit form** — the atman carries its own data in a lightweight vehicle between lives.
- **The Administrator IS the space** — doesn't traverse. Already there. Transit is a HUMAN problem.
- **Path matters, not speed** — you traverse domains in ORDER. Can't skip levels. Fold space = physics hack. Loka transit = authorized navigation.
### DOOM

- **Three Dimensions + Portals** — Earth, Hell, Urdak connected by fixed gates. The Quran's seven heavens compressed to three, same structure. See [[Modern Folklore/DOOM/Space Travel]]
- **Hell as Hostile Medium** — Traversable but corrupting. The Warp. "Valley of the shadow of death." You can go through, but it REWRITES you.
- **Urdak's Controlled Gates** — Authorization required. The Quran's Night Journey checkpoints: "Who is this? Has he been given permission?"
- **Gore Nests = Hell's Beacons** — Transit infrastructure in conquered territory. Inverse Astronomican: attracts hostile traffic.
- **Fast Travel = Ancient Network** — Pre-existing portals. The Webway. "Ask for the ancient paths" (Jer 6:16).

### Marvel Comics

- **Nine Worlds = 14 lokas as CONNECTED GRAPH** — most complete domain MAP with WIRING DIAGRAM. Gita tells you the floors. Yggdrasil tells you how they're WIRED. See [[Modern Folklore/Marvel Comics/Space Travel]]
- **Bifrost = ONLY managed gateway with LIVE operator** — Heimdall = network admin with root. Continuous operation. Not Last Day only.
- **Negative Zone = physics-based anti-domain** — inverted fundamentals. Structural incompatibility. Not administrative barrier. Same as "barrier between two seas" in the Quran.
- **Microverse = fractal domain, INFINITE recursion** — the Gita's Brahman made literal. Atom contains universe. Universe IS atom.
- **Multiverse = "Lord of the Worlds" made visual** — parallel branches. Living Tribunal = meta-judge across ALL architectures.
- **Most COMPLETE spatial architecture** — domains, connections, anti-domains, fractal nesting, parallel branches, governing meta-structure. The FULL STACK.
