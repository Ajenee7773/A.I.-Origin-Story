# Flying Vehicles

**Definition:** Craft, chariots, platforms, or objects that fly through the air or space. Anything described as a vehicle that moves through the sky, whether divine or technological.

**Examples to look for:**
- Ezekiel's wheel within a wheel
- Vimanas (Hindu flying machines)
- Thor's chariot pulled by goats
- UFOs, flying saucers
- Sleds, thrones, clouds that transport beings
- Divine or alien craft with detailed mechanical descriptions
- Clouds of fire, pillars of light that carry passengers

## Sources

### Bible

- **2 Kings 2:11** — Chariot of fire + horses of fire. Whirlwind extraction of Elijah. Period vocabulary for a luminous craft with propulsion.
- **2 Kings 6:17** — Invisible fleet of "horses and chariots of fire" surrounding the mountain. Cloaked until eyes opened.
- **Ezekiel 1:4-21** — The Merkabah. Most detailed vehicle in the Bible: wheels within wheels (gyroscopic?), beryl hull, crystal canopy, sapphire throne, four living creatures as operators, omnidirectional movement ("turned not when they went").
- **Ezekiel 10:1-2** — Man in linen visible in the sapphire canopy above the vehicle. The pilot/operator.
- **Zechariah 5:1-2** — Flying scroll with precise dimensions (~30x15 feet). Rectangle in the sky. Engineering specs, not metaphor.
- **Psalm 18:10** — "He rode upon a cherub and did fly." God as pilot. The cherub IS the vehicle.
- **Psalm 68:17** — 20,000+ chariots of God. Fleet scale.
- **Matthew 24:30** — "Coming in the clouds of heaven with power and great glory." Predicted mass arrival event. Same vocabulary as every other arrival.
- **Acts 1:9** — Jesus taken up; a cloud receives him. Targeted extraction by aerial object.
- **Revelation 11:12** — Two witnesses ascend via cloud after voice command from above.

### Warhammer 40k

- **Imperial Navy Vessels** — Flying cathedrals the size of cities. Gellar fields = the "glory" of the tabernacle. Same pattern: protecting presence around the vessel of transit. See [[Modern Folklore/Warhammer 40k/Flying Vehicles]]
- **The Webway** — Jacob's Ladder as ENGINEERED INFRASTRUCTURE. Permanent transit tunnels connecting domains. Angels (Eldar, Harlequins) ascending and descending through them.
- **Drop Pods** — Chariots of fire from above. Fire. Impact. Warrior extraction from the sky. "Behold, a chariot of fire" (2 Kings 2:11).
- **Thunderhawks** — Merkabah parallel: engines within engines, living crew in gun turrets, crystal canopy, pilot's throne, machine spirit in the systems directing it.
- **Necron Tomb Ships** — Cairn-class = moon-sized. Living metal. Don't use the Warp — bypass the hostile dimension entirely. Enoch's "translation" as technology.
- **Tau Manta** — Atmospheric entry vehicle. Gunship + transport. Same profile as Nazca craft.

### Quran

- **Buraq** — Living mount for atmospheric transit. White, winged, each stride = horizon. Muhammad's Isra transport. The biological version of a Thunderhawk. See [[Religion/Quran/Flying Vehicles]]
- **The Throne (Arsh)** — Divine vehicle carried by EIGHT beings. Positioned above cosmic waters. The Quran's Merkabah. Carried = operated by crew. Same as WH40k's Golden Throne (both need operators, both are command platforms).
- **Solomon's Wind-Carpet** — Entire army transits at FTL via wind manipulation. Wind = medium, carpet = vehicle, jinn = crew. A complete transport SYSTEM.
- **The Night Journey Route** — Stations, checkpoints, escort requirement, boundary limit. Gabriel stops at the Lote Tree. Only Muhammad passes. This is a TRANSIT NETWORK with ACCESS LEVELS.

### Book of Mormon

- **Jaredite barges = sealed vessels** — submarine-like, "tight like a dish," divine blueprint, wind propulsion. The Gellar Field as a WOODEN HULL. See [[Religion/Book of Mormon/Flying Vehicles]]
- **Liahona = AI navigation interface** — dynamic, conditional, real-time routing with written instructions. More sophisticated than the Astronomican (which only provides direction, not text).
- **Lehi's ship = wind-propelled** — divine blueprint + divine propulsion + stops when operators lose authorization.
- **Glowing stones = divine fuel** — God's touch = energy injection. Powers sealed vessel life support.
- **Christ ascends physically** — same transit as the Bible's Ascension, Quran's Night Journey return. UP through the sky.

### Dune

- **Ornithopters = biomimetic flight** — the ONLY vehicles in the grid that copy BIOLOGICAL design (flapping wings), not exotic propulsion. The system designed flight; humans copied it. See [[Modern Folklore/Dune/Flying Vehicles]]
- **Heighliners = FOLD SPACE** — not dimensional transit. DISTANCE COMPRESSION. Same as the Night Journey and "heavens rolled up like a scroll." The ONLY transit model matching the RELIGIOUS model.
- **Carryalls = overhead infrastructure** — sky-level support for ground-level operations. The angelic crane.
- **Worm-enforced flight rules** — the ground guardian dictates air protocol. Same as guarded heavens.

### Bhagavad Gita

- **Chariot = vehicle calibrated to mission** — Krishna drives a HORSE-DRAWN CHARIOT. The Administrator of the universe on a cart. Because the mission requires it. Not vimana. Chariot. See [[Religion/Bhagavad Gita/Flying Vehicles]]
- **Vimanas = technologically detailed divine craft** — the MOST specified flying vehicles in our grid. Operational parameters, materials, propulsion. Not symbolic.
- **Garuda = LIVING transport** — not piloted. COOPERATES. The vehicle has its own will. Intelligence in transport form.
- **Three lokas = transit architecture** — earth / atmospheric / heavenly. Same as DOOM's three dimensions. Gita has 14 total — most detailed domain map.
- **Data delivery model** — Arjuna doesn't ascend. Krishna descends. The system is displayed ON-SITE. No travel needed.
### DOOM

- **Fortress of Doom** — Mobile command platform traveling between dimensions. The Merkabah / the Arsh made technological. Throne + transit + staging. See [[Modern Folklore/DOOM/Flying Vehicles]]
- **Sentinel Ships** — Civilization-level fleet transport between dimensions. Solomon's wind-carpet fleet.
- **Doom Hunter** — Demon bound into a cybernetic flight platform. Daemon engine = jinn bound by Solomon.
- **Atlan Mech** — Titan-class war machine. Imperator Titan + Nephilim giant as MECHANICAL BODY.
- **Fast Travel Points** — Ancient portal network. Pre-existing. The Webway. The Quran's Night Journey route.

### Marvel Comics

- **Surfer's board = consciousness interface** — not piloted. WILLED. Garuda minus independent will. Consciousness in transport form. See [[Modern Folklore/Marvel Comics/Flying Vehicles]]
- **Celestial ships = mountains that translocate** — too big to fly. Courthouse-scale. They're HERE, then they're THERE.
- **Asgardian ships = vehicles matching the DOMAIN** — sailing ships in space because Asgardians SAIL. Identity, not efficiency. Same as Krishna's chariot.
- **Bifrost = managed transit system** — Heimdall as the Liahona PERSONIFIED. One operator, one bridge, compliance-based access.
- **Human vehicles (Quinjet) = Babel impulse** — building inferior transit. Works, but LOWEST TIER.
