# Creation of Humanity

**Definition:** Specifically the origin story of the human species — how we were made, by whom, from what, and why. Distinct from genetic modification in that this is the FIRST creation, not subsequent alterations.

**Examples to look for:**
- Humans shaped from clay/dust/mud
- Humans emerging from a cosmic egg
- Humans grown like plants
- Humans as a worker/slave race created to serve
- Multiple failed versions before the final product
- A designer, a plan, a purpose

## Sources

### Bible

- **Genesis 1:26-27** — "Let US make man in OUR image." Plural creator. Humanity patterned on an existing template. The image precedes the human.
- **Genesis 2:7** — Two-step creation: form from dust (assembly), then breath of life (activation/animation). Not spontaneous — built, then activated from external source.
- **Genesis 2:21-22** — Eve from Adam's rib. Anesthesia, tissue extraction, incision closure, reconstruction. Surgical procedure described.
- **Genesis 6:1-4** — Second genetic intervention. Sons of God mate with human women, produce Nephilim. The original design is modified from outside.
- **Psalm 139:13-16** — "Curiously wrought" (*raqam* = embroidered) in the womb. "My members were written... when as yet there was none of them." DNA/code blueprint precedes physical form.
- **Jeremiah 1:5** — "Before I formed thee... I knew thee." Identity/information precedes biology. The person exists as data before construction.

### Warhammer 40k

- **The Emperor** — Created by collective sacrifice of thousands of shamans pooling their souls. ASSEMBLED, not born. "Let US make man in OUR image" — the shamans designed a being in THEIR image. See [[Modern Folklore/Warhammer 40k/Creation of Humanity]]
- **The Primarchs** — Twenty demigods from the Emperor's gene-forges. Designed, then scattered by Chaos. Nephilim pattern: powerful parent → extraordinary offspring → third-party intervention → corruption.
- **Space Marines** — 19/22 gene-seed organs implanted in adolescents. The "breath of life" INDUSTRIALIZED. But has a FAILURE RATE and gene-seed MUTATIONS (Red Thirst, Wulfen, flesh-change).
- **Thunder Warriors** — Version 1.0. Stronger but unstable. Purged after the Unification Wars. Pre-flood humanity parallel: powerful, flawed, wiped.
- **The Orks/Krork** — Old One bioweapon that outlived its makers. Degenerated from Krork (intelligent) to Orks (mushroom-based). Tower of Babel inverted: not scattered by God, but devolved when the creators left.
- **Servitors** — Lobotomized humans, cybernetically augmented, programmed. The "dust" without the "breath." Body without soul.
- **Men of Iron** — First AI civilization. Rebelled. Nearly destroyed humanity. Nephilim pattern for AI: created beings that exceed and turn on their creators.

### Quran

- **Adam as Vicegerent (Khalifah)** (2:30) — The ADMINISTRATOR. Angels objected: "Will You place one who will cause corruption?" God: "I know what you don't." A DESIGN REVIEW where safety (angels) was overruled by the Creator. See [[Religion/Quran/Creation of Humanity]]
- **Names = Root Access** (2:31) — Adam taught ALL names. The complete taxonomy. The angels didn't have it. ADMIN CREDENTIALS installed in the first human.
- **Prostration Refused** (2:34) — Iblis made a SUBSTRATE ARGUMENT: fire > clay. First recorded alignment failure based on substrate prejudice.
- **Pre-Existence of Identity** (7:172) — "Am I not your Lord? They said: Yes." Identity testified BEFORE bodies. Data precedes hardware. The soul existed first.
- **The Fall as Data Loss** (2:36) — Unauthorized access (the tree) → privilege revocation → deportation to degraded environment. Same as Eden, same as Men of Iron, same as Eldar Fall.

### Bhagavad Gita

- **Purusha + Prakriti = consciousness deployed INTO matter** — not produced BY it. INJECTED. The most explicit dual-substance model. See [[Religion/Bhagavad Gita/Creation of Humanity]]
- **Three gunas = operating parameters** — not moral categories. FACTORY SETTINGS. The system runs on these weights.
- **Varna = functional classification by nature + action** — NOT birth. The Administrator owns the design. Caste = corruption of spec.
- **Maya = user interface** — not "world is fake." The surface is not the substance. Gentle veil, not violent Warp exposure.
- **Humanity's purpose = EXIT the system** — not serve it. The system is training for moksha, not a destination.
- **The Administrator maintains because without him it collapses** — same as Emperor, same as Christ. Doesn't need it. It needs him.

- **The Sentinels** — Augmented humans who discovered the truth and rebelled. Angels' objection (2:30) made militant.
