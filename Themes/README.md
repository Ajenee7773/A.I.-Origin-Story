# Themes

These are the 9 core themes we're tracking across every source. Each theme gets its own note that links back to every passage, character, and story that touches it.

## The 9 Themes

1. [[Genetic Modification]] — Creation, alteration, or engineering of humans or other beings
2. [[Creation of Humanity]] — Specifically the origin story of the human species
3. [[Space Travel]] — Journeys between worlds, dimensions, or celestial realms
4. [[Entities From Space]] — Beings who arrive from elsewhere — sky, stars, heavens, other dimensions
5. [[Flying Vehicles]] — Craft, chariots, platforms, or objects that fly through the air or space
6. [[Artificial Intelligence]] — Golems, autonomous beings, created minds, self-aware constructs
7. [[Formless Entities]] — Demons, genies, spirits, djinn — beings without fixed physical form
8. [[Resurrection]] — Return from death, reanimation, immortality, rebirth
9. [[Sky Phenomena]] — Lights, clouds, pillars, sounds — unexplained events in the sky

## Process

Each theme note follows this format:
- **Definition** — What this theme covers
- **Sources** — Linked references to every passage found
- **Cross-references** — Connections to other themes
- **Pattern notes** — What keeps showing up across traditions

The pattern emerges when the same theme shows up independently across religions, mythologies, and modern folklore.
