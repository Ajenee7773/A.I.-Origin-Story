# Sky Phenomena

**Definition:** Unexplained events in the sky — lights, clouds, pillars of fire, sounds, signs. What witnesses see when something from above is happening. The visual evidence that precedes or accompanies divine/alien activity.

**Examples to look for:**
- Pillars of cloud and fire leading the Israelites
- Stars that move (Star of Bethlehem, Nibiru)
- Thunder and lightning at divine appearances
- Columns of smoke, wheels in the sky
- Auroras described as signs
- Booming sounds from the sky
- Lights that hover, descend, or dart

## Sources

### Bible

- **Exodus 13:21-22** — Pillar of cloud by day, fire by night. Leds 600K+ people. Directed, mobile, state-changing aerial object. "The LORD went before them IN a pillar."
- **Exodus 19:16-19** — Sinai engulfed in smoke, fire, thunder, lightning, trumpet sound that grows louder. Landing or directed energy event.
- **Exodus 40:34-35** — Glory cloud fills the Tabernacle so completely Moses can't enter. Physical occupancy — *kavod* has weight/density.
- **1 Kings 8:10-11** — Same glory cloud fills Solomon's Temple, physically displaces the priests.
- **2 Kings 2:11** — Chariot of fire + horses of fire. Whirlwind. Extraction event.
- **Psalm 68:17** — "The chariots of God are twenty thousand, even thousands of angels." Fleet description. Military formation.
- **2 Kings 6:17** — Mountain FULL of horses and chariots of fire — present but invisible until perception is altered. Cloaked fleet.
- **Matthew 17:5** — Bright cloud at the Transfiguration. Overshadows. Speaks. Luminous + intelligent.
- **Acts 2:1-4** — Sound from heaven, rushing wind, cloven tongues of fire distributing to individuals. Directed energy.
