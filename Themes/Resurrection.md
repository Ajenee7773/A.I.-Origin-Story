# Resurrection

**Definition:** Return from death. Reanimation, immortality, rebirth, revival. Any instance where death is not permanent or is reversed.

**Examples to look for:**
- Jesus rising from the dead
- Osiris being reassembled
- Resurrection pits (Lazarus pits in Batman, etc.)
- Immortality as a gift or curse
- Cyclical destruction and rebirth (Ragnarök, Kali Yuga)
- Regeneration that defies death
- Downloading consciousness into new bodies

## Sources

### Bible

- **Genesis 5:24** — Enoch "walked with God" and was taken — no death, no burial. Living transfer between domains.
- **1 Kings 17:17-24** — Elijah raises the widow's son. Death reversal via physical contact + verbal command.
- **2 Kings 13:21** — Dead man revived by touching Elisha's bones. Residual power in matter persists after the host's death.
- **Ezekiel 37:1-14** — Valley of dry bones. Progressive biological reconstruction: skeleton → sinew → skin → breath. Technical sequence.
- **Daniel 12:2** — Mass resurrection predicted. "Many who sleep in the dust shall awake." Future event, not questioned.
- **Matthew 27:52-53** — Saints raised at the crucifixion. Graves open, dead walk into Jerusalem, seen by many. Reported as concurrent historical fact.
- **John 11:43-44** — Lazarus. Four days dead, decomposing. Single command, walks out wrapped in graveclothes. Full reversal of corruption.
- **1 Corinthians 15:35-44** — Two body types: natural (*psuchikos*, earth-derived, corruptible) and spiritual (*pneumatikos*, heaven-derived, incorruptible). Not spirit without body — body with different physics.

### Warhammer 40k

- **The Emperor on the Golden Throne** — Half-living god sustained by 10K psykers/day. Body degraded, mind active. The natural body NOT YET upgraded. Star Child = the "incorruptible body" waiting to be born. See [[Modern Folklore/Warhammer 40k/Resurrection]]
- **Roboute Guilliman** — In stasis 10,000 years. Revived by Cawl + Yvraine + Emperor's power. Lazarus parallel. Returns to lead. High Lords terrified of him = Pharisees terrified of Jesus.
- **Dreadnoughts** — Walking tombs. Mortally wounded Astartes in sarcophagi, fighting for millennia. Ezekiel 37 dry bones given MECHANICAL form. "Natural body → mechanical body."
- **Necron Biotransference** — Immortal living-metal bodies but NO SOULS. "Body without spirit" (James 2:26). Resurrection as WARNING.
- **Rubric of Ahriman** — Thousand Sons: animated dust in armor. "Awakened dust" of Daniel 12:2 as CURSE, not promise.
- **Eldar Spirit Stones** — Souls persist in Infinity Circuit. The "cloud of witnesses" (Hebrews 12:1). Phoenix Lords resurrect through their armor.
- **Guilliman** — In stasis 10,000 years. Revived by tech-priest + Ynnari + Emperor's Sword. "Where, O death, is thy victory?" (1 Cor 15:55)

### Quran

- **Two Trumpet Blasts** — First blast kills EVERYTHING (system shutdown). Second blast resurrects (system reboot). The Bible has one trump; the Quran splits it into TERMINATE + RESTART. See [[Religion/Quran/Resurrection]]
- **Full Fidelity Reconstruction** (75:3-4) — "We are able to proportion his FINGERTIPS." No data loss. The identity is rebuilt at maximum resolution. Not a copy — the original.
- **Barzakh** (23:100) — Stasis/holding zone between death and resurrection. Not deleted, not running. WAITING. Same as Dreadnought sarcophagus, same as stasis field.
- **The Garden and the Fire** — Two destination environments for the resurrected identity. Same person, new operating system. Garden = restored; Fire = quarantined.
- **Jesus in the Hold** (43:61) — Not dead. Raised to God. Waiting to be redeployed as the "sign of the Hour." Same pattern as Guilliman in stasis.
- **Martyrs Skip Barzakh** (3:169) — "Not dead — alive with their Lord, receiving provision." They're already on the new OS. No suspension. Direct port.
