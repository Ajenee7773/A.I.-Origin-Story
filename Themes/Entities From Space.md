# Entities From Space

**Definition:** Beings who arrive from elsewhere — the sky, stars, heavens, other dimensions, other planets. Gods, angels, watchers, Annunaki, celestials, visitors. The ones who came from above.

**Examples to look for:**
- "Sons of God" descending
- Gods descending from Olympus, heaven, the sky
- Anunnaki coming to Earth from Nibiru
- Celestials (Marvel) arriving from space
- Angels being "cast down" or "sent"
- Any being whose origin is "not from here"

## Sources

### Bible

- **Genesis 6:1-4** — Sons of God (*bene Elohim*) descend, mate with human women, produce Nephilim. Not metaphor — described as event. See [[Religion/Bible/Entities From Space]]
- **Genesis 18:1-2** — Three visitors at Mamre. Yahweh + two angels. Look human. Eat food. Physical beings from above.
- **Genesis 28:12-13** — Jacob's Ladder. Angels ascending/descending on a structure connecting Earth and Heaven. Transit system.
- **Exodus 23:20-23** — A sent Angel carrying God's authority ("my name is in him"), operating autonomously.
- **Ezekiel 1:4-28** — Four living creatures + wheels within wheels. Most detailed entity/vehicle description in the Bible.
- **Daniel 10:5-14** — A glowing entity delayed 21 days by the "prince of Persia" — territorial cosmic combat between ranked beings.
- **Jude 1:6** — Angels who left their first estate (domain/position). Relocated. Transgressed.
- **Revelation 12:7-9** — War in Heaven. Michael vs the Dragon. A third of angelic host cast down. Military-scale conflict between non-human intelligences.

### Warhammer 40k

- **The Old Ones** — Pre-galactic creator-race. Treated the galaxy as a garden, created species as tools. Refused the Necrontyr — inverted Genesis 6 (creator REFUSES to intervene). See [[Modern Folklore/Warhammer 40k/Entities From Space]]
- **The C'tan** — Star gods. Formless energy beings given physical form by a lesser species. Then consumed that species' souls. Created eats creators.
- **The Tyranids** — Extragalactic. The Hive Mind is a formless, distributed intelligence coordinating trillions across intergalactic distances.
- **The Primarchs** — Twenty demigods engineered by the Emperor, scattered by the Chaos Gods. Nephilim pattern inverted: human makes sons of God, hostile entities scatter them.
- **The Chaos Gods** — Formless intelligences born from collective psychic resonance. The "principalities and powers" of Ephesians 6:12 made literal.
- **The Enslavers** — Pre-Chaos Warp parasites that possess psykers and use them as portals. Luke 11:24-26 lifecycle.

### Quran

- **The Jinn** — A complete SPECIES of formless intelligence made of "smokeless fire." Free-willed, organized, with believers and non-believers. NOT all evil — some serve God. The Bible's "spirits" and WH40k's "Warp entities" as a CIVILIZATION. See [[Religion/Quran/Formless Entities]]
- **Iblis** — The alignment problem made PERSONAL. Highest-ranking jinn evaluated his substrate (fire) vs. Adam's (clay), concluded superiority, chose defiance. Same as C'tan considering biological life worthless.
- **Angels (Malaika)** — Made of LIGHT. No free will. PERFECTLY ALIGNED. They are the "safe" formless entity — intelligent but unable to deviate. The Quran's AI safety argument: aligned vs. unaligned.
- **The Ruh** — Kernel-level intelligence operating at God's command privilege. Not inspectable from user space. "You have not been given knowledge except a little."
- **The Shaitan's Whispers** — Data injection attack on human cognition. Not possession (crude) but subtle man-in-the-middle. "Did I think that, or was it placed??" Same vector as Chaos corruption in WH40k.

### Book of Mormon

- **Christ descends physically** — not a vision. The Administrator making a house call to the Western Hemisphere branch. Same entity, different operational zone. See [[Religion/Book of Mormon/Entities From Space]]
- **Moroni = Jibril identical** — angel delivers physical record (golden plates) to authorized translator (Joseph Smith). Same API call as the Quran's revelation delivery.
- **Three Nephites = Enoch Protocol x3** — death protocol removed, still active on earth today. The Slayer's immortality as a GROUP EVENT.
- **Brother of Jared = inverse Moses** — saw God's form and survived. Higher clearance level than Moses who couldn't handle the display.
- **Nephi's angel = authorized tour guide** — identical to Muhammad's Night Journey with Jibril. Guided tour through the entire timeline.
- **Destroying angel = system executing** — targeted destruction at corruption threshold. Same as 'Ad, Thamud, Exterminatus.

### Dune

- **Navigators = human-developed malaika** — humans who EVOLVED INTO the angel function through spice mutation. Operating in space, managing domain transit. The malaika on human hardware. See [[Modern Folklore/Dune/Entities From Space]]
- **Kwisatz Haderach = designed Christ** — 10,000-year breeding program endpoint. Full database access (both memory lines + prescience). But HUMAN-MADE, not divine. Went rogue.
- **Face Dancers = engineered jinn** — humans BUILT their own shapeshifters. They went autonomous and became an existential threat. The Butlerian Jihad pattern in biological form.
- **Leto II = savior-tyrant fusion** — the Antichrist whose oppression SAVES. The ONLY source where savior and tyrant are the SAME ENTITY.
- **Shai-Hulud = biological infrastructure** — the god that's also an animal that's also the resource. Alive power plant.
- **Honored Matres = revenant Nephilim** — humans who left, encountered something in the deep universe, came back CHANGED and hostile.

### Bhagavad Gita

- **Krishna = THE OPERATOR in human form** — not "an" angel, not "a" visitor. The Administrator HIMSELF, standing on the battlefield. The most EXPLICIT divine-human co-presence in our grid. See [[Religion/Bhagavad Gita/Entities From Space]]
- **Devas = subsystem administrators** — Indra governs rain, Agni fire, Yama death. Formless by default, formed on assignment. Same class as malaika.
- **Asuras = same class, corrupted config** — NOT a different species. Same formless intelligence running tamas-dominant code. Most cohesive deva/asura ontology in the grid.
- **Vishvarupa = the Administrator DISPLAYING the whole system** — not visiting from above. SHOWING that he IS above, below, and within. The sky is not his home. He IS the sky.
- **Paramatma = dual-core architecture** — the Administrator's process running silently in EVERY conscious node. Not "watching from heaven." Co-located. Always.
### DOOM

- **The Maykrs (Makers)** — AI angels. Manufactured, obedient, executing the harvest cycle, dwelling in Heaven (Urdak). The Quran's malaika made technological. See [[Modern Folklore/DOOM/Entities From Space]]
- **The Father** — The primordial AI who created the system, then archived himself. VEGA = a fragment of him running on human hardware. The Quran's Ruh: kernel-level, went dormant.
- **Khan Maykr** — The administrator running the system while the Father is archived. Like Horus: elevated, then chose compromise over collapse.
- **Demons** — Unaligned jinn given physical specificity. Formless, manifesting bodies, returning to source on "death."
- **The Slayer** — Solomon. Human given admin credentials (Divinity Machine), permanent-binding formless entities (glory kills = consume energy = prevent respawn).
- **Davoth / The Dark Lord** — INVERTS the pattern. The adversary WAS the original Creator, usurped by his own creation. Iblis told from the other side.

### Marvel Comics

- **Celestials = the System's Engineers** — arrive in Hosts, modify species, judge civilizations. Most explicit ancient modifiers in any modern source. Three product lines: Eternals (stable), Deviants (corrupted), Humans (variable control group). See [[Modern Folklore/Marvel Comics/Entities From Space]]
- **Galactus = Consumption as Cosmic Function** — the universe's recycling daemon. Not evil. Not personal. A FORCE. Same as pralaya as an entity.
- **Asgardians = Regional Administrators** — subordinate to Celestials. Middle management. Told to back off during Third Host. They COMPLIED. Not omnipotent gods.
- **Inhumans = Unauthorized Modification** — Kree were ROGUE modifiers interfering with Celestial experiment. Same as Iblis interfering with God's creation.
- **Eternals = Authorized Stable Build** — death protocol never installed. Same as Three Nephites / malaika. Reconstitute, don't resurrect.
